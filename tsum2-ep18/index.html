Interactions with Kalim Tsum II

Interior Hallway

Kalim
Man~ We sure feasted back there! Jamil's cooking really is delicious after all.
Jamil
Who would've thought that we'd bump into Azul's lot? For them to just hand over such good quality ingredients so liberally... We're sure that they won't demand repayment from us later on, right?

*Happy Tsum*

Kalim
Ohh, so you had fun too, Tsum! You did dance along with Jamil's movements as he was cooking just now and all.
Now that we're stuffed... Jamil, keep us company for one more place!

Sports Field

Kalim
We're here, Tsum!

*Happy Tsum*

Jamil
I didn't think that we'd go to the sports field for our final destination. I don't think there's anything that we have to bring the Tsum all the way out here specifically to see though...
Kalim
Nope, there definitely is. If we ride this fella, that is.

*Magic carpet*

*Excited Tsum*

Jamil
The magic carpet? When did you...
Kalim
I came up with the idea while you were cooking, so I got the dorm guys to bring it over.
Hop on it, Tsum. And you too, Jamil. I'm gonna show you how the school looks from up in the sky!

*Tsum boarding*

Kalim
...Aight, looks like we're all up. Then it's time for take off! Hang on tight, everyone!

Night Raven College

Kalim
How's it, Tsum? Our school's really huge, right!
So that's the botanical garden, and that one all the way behind it is the Ramshackle Dorm. The building you can see right in front is the Mirror Chamber we came from.

*Happy Tsum*

Kalim
Ahaha! Nice view, right? We'll be going around the school once like this!

*Happy Tsum*

Jamil
We're making a loop round the school in the end, huh...
...Well, this should be less tedious than walking the entire way anyway. Guess I'll just let this slide.

Sky

Kalim
Alright... It was quite rushed, but this means we managed to get the school tour done at least.
How was it, Tsum? Did you have fun?

*Tsum dance*

Kalim
Ooohh, it was so fun that you're breaking into dance? I'm really happy too!
Be careful though, Tsum? If you fall from this height...

*Zwip!*

*Tsum disappear*

Kalim

AH! LOOK OUT!

Kalim grabs Tsum

Jamil

EEK!!!

Jamil grabs Kalim grabbing Tsum

Kalim
Alright, gotcha! Man, Tsum, you're so careless~

*Happy Tsum*

Kalim
Also, thanks Jamil for holding me in! I felt myself almost slip right there!
Jamil

YOU'RE CARELESS TOO, MIND YOU!

Seriously... You really keep me on tenterhooks all the way to the end. It's time you brought us back to land.
Kalim
Gotcha. Okay Tsum, we're heading back to ground level!

*Happy Tsum*

Sports Field

Kalim
And we've arrived~! Be careful when you get down, Tsum.

*Tsum gets down*

Kalim
So how was today for you, Tsum?

*Happy Tsum*

Kalim
Is that so! I was also able to create some great memories to remember.
Did you manage to enjoy yourself too, Jamil?
Jamil
Well... Right. It's much more palatable than sneaking out of the dorm without a word at least.
Kalim
Is that so! Looks like everyone had fun! Great!
Then... Tsum, Jamil! Let's go back to Scarabia!

*Happy Tsum*
