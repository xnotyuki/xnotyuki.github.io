Posted on 25th Jun 2021 (Friday) Edited on 12th Dec 2023 (Tuesday) In Twisted Wonderland , Event Story , Master Chef (Spicy)

Title: Let’s Hear Your Thoughts
Characters: Kalim, Trey, Chef Ghost

Click here to view the Prologue!

Cafeteria - Judging Venue

Head Chef
Right. Now that we have taught you the basics of cooking…
Let’s hear your thoughts on the Master Chef lessons so far from the two of you.
Kalim
I couldn’t progress much because I wasn’t familiar with the work and was stunned at every turn… I experienced firsthand how much more difficult cooking is than I thought.
Kalim
When I watched Jamil chop vegetables with the kitchen knife and the like, it looked so much easier~
Kalim
But thanks to the lessons here, I’m picking up recipes to prepare for my guests next time!
Trey
I joined this program to learn the secrets to teaching others how to cook and…
Trey
The chefs’ anecdotes of how beginners fumble in the kitchen make for good references.
Trey
That, and watching Kalim cook.
Kalim
Eh, why me?
Trey
You mentioned you have almost no experience with cooking, right? How you reacted during the classes was helpful for me.
Kalim
I see~! I didn’t think you would have time in the kitchen to notice such details. People like you who are used to kitchen work are so amazing.
Kalim
I’ll keep practising so that I can become just like Trey.
Head Chef
Hehehe! Both of you seem to have learnt from each other during the lessons, so that’s great!
Kalim
Alright, let’s have fun for the remaining classes too!
Trey
Yeah, let’s do our best to whip up dish after dish so that everyone will say it’s delicious.
