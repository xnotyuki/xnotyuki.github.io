The snow’s piled up a fair bit today too, so I’ve made plans to go tidy up the Magift court with the other Magift club members.

The weather’s all fine and dandy with me, but the other club members were already looking spent even yesterday… Pretty pathetic of 'em, don’t you agree?

I’m thinking of completing the assignments we were given earlier by the end of the day. After all, that’s just the natural thing to do to an honours student, right? But…

You’re saying…it’s full of really hard applied knowledge exercises all the way from question 1? Aight! I’m gonna pump myself up and get down to it!

Of course I’m gonna stuff today full of online games just like any other day… There’s just no way I’d go out on such a chilly day like this on the other hand, you know?

I upgraded the dorm’s net connection recently so it’s been super fast and it’s the absolute GOAT lmao~ Tis the season for the first all-nighter gaming marathon in a long time!

Since everyday is cold now, I was thinking of preparing some ginger lemonade concentrate or something. One sip and it’ll warm you up through and through.

What, having lemonade after 8 is banned under the rules of the Queen of Hearts?1 But it being ginger lemonade makes it a whole other thing, doesn’t it?

I will be waiting for one and all to patronise the Mostro Lounge as usual.

On such a cold day, we would introduce a selection of winter specials on our menu. Today is looking to be a busy one indeed.

The Track and Field Club’s got club activities today. But it’ll probably wind up being indoor training since the weather report said it’ll snow in the afternoon.

It takes time to keep myself warm during winter, so I’m gonna have to put in everything I’ve got into the warm ups.

I’ll be going to pick up the spring coat that I’d ordered. While I’m at it, I’ll also look for accessories that pair well with it.

If I begin preparations only after temperatures begin climbing, then all I can do at that point is kiss the season goodbye.

Since there’s no club activities for today, I plan to look some stuff up. I’m planning to travel with my family during the holidays, you see.

Sightseeing spots, climate info, local delicacies, souvenirs… No harm having more information at my fingertips, don’t you agree?

Nothing worth mentioning. There, done. Not what you’d hoped for? Well that’s too bad.

Snow’s piling up all over the school anyway, so of course nothing’d beat hurrying back to my dorm room and chilling out with some chess problems.

Today is quite a chilly day. Such a day is best spent keeping warm in my room and enjoying some frozen desserts.

Shall I ask Lilia and the rest to join me afterwards? Hehe… Being the one making arrangements for the others once in a while certainly isn’t bad.

The new game I preordered online just arrived, so I’ll probably spend today gaming with my roommates.

Owning physical copies of games does mean that they take up much needed room, but when it comes to a release you really like, you’d wanna keep it within reach in your very own collection, wouldn’t you?

I will be receiving swordsmanship training from Father afterwards. I’d planned on finishing up my assignments before that but…

It seems like I dozed off at some point since my notes are completely blank. This is quite the rut…

Think I’m gonna check out the winter-exclusive Magicammable spots on Magicam, maybe ☆

You know, stuff like diamond dust, hotels carved out of ice and all that… That seasonal special kinda feeling’s got that rarity oomph to it that really gets the mood up, doesn’t it ♪

I saw someone wearing some really fluffy boots yesterday and it got me interested, so I was just thinkin’ of heading down to Foothill Town to look for 'em.

Wonder if that’s hard to walk in? I’ll buy a pair and try it on for myself if there’s anything that looks good.

Today? It’s snowing outside, plus I’ve got no club activities for the day, so I was thinking of watching the movies and dramas that looked interesting in my subs list or something.

I’ll do that while munching on snacks and stuff in my warm and toasty room. Having days like this once in a while ain’t bad either, don’tcha think?

I plan to check on the houseplants I’m growing at the Science Club.

Hehe… Just what kind of expressions will they grace me with today, I wonder? I do look forward to seeing it change in a new way from yesterday.

Uuughh, it’s freezing~! Oi, minion! We’re gonna lounge around in that KOTATSU thing today.

We’ll also chow down on some snacks and play games together… Myahaha! Today feels like it’ll be the best day ever!

The Pop Music Club has band practice today. But you see… It’s so cold now that my fingers are numb all the way through and it’s oh, so unbearable.

Hm? Why, the music score is already up here in my head. All that’s left is to play together with the other members as one!

I’m thinking of making my way to the library today. The book I borrowed yesterday has scribbles all over its pages, you see.

My plan is to search for a clean copy there to swap with since these scrawls are distracting to me. Really now… Scribbling in a book of all things is simply a felony.

A peddler from the Scalding Sands is popping by today. I’m gonna go and buy a whole bunch of stuff for the holidays!

I’m practically shaking with excitement thinking how there might be excavated stuff to check out! Bring your friends along to check their wares out too!

Of course I’m gonna spend it on part time jobs! Today I’ll be at a cake store, and tomorrow I’m gonna be waitering at a restaurant…

Since the holidays are approaching, it’s the prime time for scoring tons of cash from high-paying part-time jobs! Shishishi!

I was considering making herbaria since the weather is arid around this time of the year. The conditions are simply perfect.

If you would like, why don’t you join me in making them? Fret not, for I will be sure to teach you the ins and outs of the craft.

I intend to read the book I’d ordered for pickup at the Mystery Shop the other day. It’s one that I’d been looking forward to obtaining.

My esteemed grandfather had recommended it to me, so I have to read it a few times over and inform him of what I thought of it!

Notes

  1. This is probably the same rule as in Book 1-14 of the Main Story, but the wording here is vaguer than that one (Book 1’s specified 8pm, and it’s lemonade with honey that’s banned, while Trey only mentions lemonade generally, and doesn’t specify if it’s AM or PM here). Left it as vague as it is just in case.
