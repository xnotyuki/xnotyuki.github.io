Getting in the Mood

Scalding Sands - Kalim’s House

Kalim
Yeah! We actually had traditional Scalding Sands outfits prepared for everyone.
Trey, Cater and Malleus
Huh!?
Kalim
I was discussing with Jamil yesterday about how we could make today memorable for you guys.
Jamil
I had thought it’d be too intrusive to do so… but since all of you did take the time out specially to visit the Scalding Sands…
We prepared them with the hopes that it could enable everyone to have a sense of the Scalding Sands’ history without a sliver of doubt-1
As well as for you to enjoy the Yasmina River Fireworks Festival.
Malleus
So this was the “surprise” that Asim had mentioned earlier? How meticulous of you.
Cater
And what a surprise indeed! I can’t thank you two enough for how overjoyed this makes me!
Trey
It’s been stuffy being here in our uniforms anyway, so I appreciate that.
Kalim
Go get changed too, Jamil. Guide the rest too while you’re on your way.
I’ll be waiting here with Grim anyway.
Jamil
Got it.
Grim
Hm? Why must I stay here and wait?
Kalim
Well, that’s…
Jamil
Because we don’t have clothes that fit you, Grim. Sorry.
Grim
Wh-What did you just-?! Fnaaaaaaagh!!
Jamil
It’s not like we had any choice, right? With how different your measurements are from the rest, it was simply impossible for us to prepare clothes that could accommodate you in addition to the rest in a single day.
Grim
Liar! You obviously forgot about my honourable attendance! I wanna wear cool clothes like the rest too, yanno!
Kalim
Don’t get so mad. Look, you can chill out in the air conditioned car in the meantime.
We also have loads of juice and snacks in there!
Grim
Ohh! In that case, I’ll very kindly let you off! Hurry up, let me eat!
Cater
Gri-gri would probably be happier that way.
Jamil
Now, everyone. I shall guide you to the fitting rooms.
Ah, right. The attendants will be carrying your luggage for you, so please entrust your belongings to them.
Trey
…As someone born to an average household, I’m not used to such extravagant treatment and it makes me restless…

Grim
Okay! Get back once you’re done, ‘cuz I’ll be having a feast now!
Grim
Kalim! Where’s the snacks?!
Trey
Looks like Grim has already shifted his focus to eating.
Grim
Om nom…
This churro-looking fried donut2 is cold and delicious!
Kalim
I love that too. Since syrup is poured over it, it’s really sweet and delicious, yeah.
Grim
It goes perfectly with the strong iced tea. Om nom…
Kalim
There’s still lots of yummy food out in the streets, so make sure to eat as much as you can then!
Grim
Okay! You can always count on me to eat!
Trey
Kept you waiting, Kalim, but I’ve finished changing.

Trey
You really surprised me. I didn’t think you’d prepare such a lavish outfit for me.
Kalim
Ohh! It suits you really well, Trey!

Cater
I’m getting in the mood just by wearing this~ It’s such a wonderful outfit ♪
I ended up taking lots and lots of selfies ‘cause it’s just so photogenic!

Malleus
Being able to wear another nation’s traditional costume… This is a valuable experience and it thrills me to my core.
Cater
Even though we’re all wearing the same designs, it’s so interesting how a change of colour is all it takes to give off different impressions.
Plus… Seeing Malleus wear something that’s not black feels kinda new ♪
Malleus
Is there anything strange about my attire?
Cater
Nuh uh~ It actually suits you to a T.
It never crossed my mind before, but it’s just fitting that Malleus is able to dress so sharply even in vivid colours!
Malleus
Hehe, flattery or not, that is certainly pleasant to the ear. Such a brilliant colour on yours suits you very well too, Diamond.
It is bright just like a firework and eye-catching.
Cater
Ehhh… Are you really saying all that with such a straight face, Malleus? I might even get a little abashed if you compliment me that much.
But it does make me super happy. Thanks ♪
…Oh, right. Earth to the Trey there who’s looking kinda embarrassed. Goes without saying that you’re absolutely slaying it too ☆
Trey
Hahaha…thanks.
Kalim
I’m glad everyone looks so happy!
Jamil
It’s been a while since I last wore our traditional attire too.

Jamil
It certainly feels as if I’d returned to my homeland after a long journey abroad.
Trey, Cater and Malleus
Whoa~!!
Trey
In the end, it fits Jamil the best out of all of us.
Malleus
I could barely recognise you, Viper.
Cater
Plus Jamil’s outfit is clearly way more gorgeous than ours! You’d get loads of likes~ ♪
Jamil
I-If all of you look at me that much, it’ll be uncomf-…
Enough with me! Let’s wait for Yuu to finish changing.
Kalim
How nice~ If only I could also stay with everyone later.
Jamil
You do have an important responsibility of making your rounds to greet the festival’s staff afterwards
Since you were elected to take up the honourable role of dormitory leader in the prestigious Night Raven College–
Master told you to wear your dorm leader outfit later so that the public announcement of your appointment can made, right?
Kalim
I meaaaan, that’s true… But…

Kalim
Oh, it suits Yuu too! It looks great!
Grim
It’s a given that an underling of mine would look cool. I’ll give you a pass in looks at least!
Jamil
I’d made all the necessary arrangements to ensure it, after all.
Trey
Just how did you get all of our measurements… Should’ve expected nothing less of Jamil.
