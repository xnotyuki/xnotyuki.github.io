Notes

  1. Tama-Friends/Tama Tomo is an actual Tamagotchi term to mean best friends. Mametchi and the others will also refer to each other as たまとも in their respective side stories.　(Return to text)

  1. Rui lists as an example how to turn off the device here, which is 切り方. Kuchipatchi misunderstands it as “how to cut” here because 切り means that too. Thankfully this pun is simple enough to translate.　(Return to text)

  1. As the name implies, Mamapatchi is Kuchipatchi’s mom.　(Return to text)
