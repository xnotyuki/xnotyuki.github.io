The Tsums Invade! II

Main Street

Lilia
Gasp…! Hunker down, Kalim! Something's flying towards us!
Kalim
What do you mean by- Dwuh-?!
Kalim

Urgh! UWAaaaaAAAAAAAAAHHHHHHHHH!!!

*SMACK!!!*

Lilia
Ooh, was that too late? That was a head shot right there. You doing alright, Kalim?
Kalim
Owie~… What exactly crashed into me?

Kalim
What's this? A pillow? No wait, is it a plushie? Its hair looks white and it has a bandanna wrapped around its head…
It feels similar to me for some reason. Ahaha!

*Squirmy squirm squirm!!!*

Kalim
WhaaaAAA?! I-It moved!! What's with this thing?!
Lilia
Oh? This round body and short legs must mean…
Kalim
Do you know what it is, Lilia?
Lilia
Why, yes. Sebek brought a being which looked very much like this one to our dorm once.
Kalim
I see… Huh? Hey, Lilia, could that thing behind you possibly be…?
Lilia
Hm?

Lilia
Ohh, there was another one here? I must say, it has an exceptionally cute appearance.
Kalim
You're right! Its hair colour and the way it jumps is just like you!
Kalim
They're such mysterious little critters~ What are they called?
Lilia
Ahh, if I recall correctly these are…

*Phone rings*

Kalim
I got a text…? AH! That reminds me, I totally forgot about contacting Jamil!
Lilia
That's because these two fell out of the sky right as you were about to do so.
Kalim
Uhhh… Where did I keep it again? Phone, phone…

*Kalim Tsum dancing*

Lilia
Ooh, it looks as if it's having fun. Is it perhaps dancing to your message ringtone?
Kalim
It really is! It's actually doing dance steps! Looks like it's a kind of critter that enjoys dancing.
Kalim
So that means the one that looks like Lilia also… Huh?

Kalim
I coulda sworn it was here just now… Where did it go?

*Footsteps*

Azul
Oh my…and I was just wondering who it was over here. If it isn't Kalim and Lilia.
Kalim
Ooh, Azul! What's up?
Azul
Well, I was just planning to go to the headmaster's office right now.
And that thing by your feet is…? I see, so it appears that you have encountered them, Kalim.
Was this individual that resembles you the only one that was spotted in this area?
Lilia
Nope, there's actually one more. Look, it's right there.
Azul
Where exactly is…"there"?

*Rustle…*

Azul
WHAAAA?! What's this?! Something soft just dropped onto my head!
Kalim
Ah, it's the fella that looks like Lilia! So that's where it went~
Lilia
Kehehe… Impressive. It had sensed Azul's presence and hid itself before proceeding to show up using a sneak attack.
Well, all it actually did was climb up a tree and drop down onto his head though.
Azul
Really now… What an unnecessary fright that was.
That aside, this is a mere proposal, but would the two of you like to visit the headmaster's office together with me?
Kalim
Sure! But also, what are you going there for at this hour?
Azul
Actually, I encountered the creature that resembles myself just a while ago… This is the one.

Kalim
Oh, it really does! It looks a lot like you! This fella's also small and cute!
Azul
Although I have had interactions with one that resembled Floyd previously, I too do not have any detailed information about these creatures.
Which is why I was contemplating finding out as much as I can from the headmaster and then seeking his advice on the next course of action.
Lilia
Hmm… It seems like something very entertaining is happening for some reason. Got it, I shall accompany you as well.
Kalim
I'll send Jamil a text to let him know I'll be back late, then… There!
I'm ready now! Azul, Lilia! Let's go to the headmaster's office!
