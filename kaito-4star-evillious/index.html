Kaito Evillious Collab 4* Side Story

Posted on 31st Dec 2021 (Friday) Edited on 13th Dec 2023 (Wednesday) In Project Sekai , Side Story , KAITO , Evillious Chronicles Collab

The Evillious collab in proseka was possibly one of the most exciting collabs for me since I followed the series in its early days (back when Re_birthday was considered the end of the Daughter of Evil series lmao) and I was hyped to see how it would be handled in the game. Quite possibly the most fun I had tling so far and I hope you enjoy the stories!

Shout out to Ylimegirl for reading through all the translations I did for the collab and helping out with lore tidbits in the series!

Also I will try to leave some notes wherever possible, especially for those who are unfamiliar with the terms used in Evillious.

Notes

  1. 冥界 usually means the Underworld, but in this context it refers to the Hellish Yard. It has a “heaven” counterpart, known as the Heavenly Yard. Hence, “Master of the Hellish Yard” instead of “Master of the Underworld” as well.
    As a subnote, the Master of the Hellish Yard has a name, she is Nemesis Sudou.　(Return to text)

  1. Yeah she really does only speak in blanks (or ellipses) in the story the entire time. I tried to compensate by faking some possibly dialogue and matching the number of underscores with letters in each word i came up with. although ngl, i dont actually remember what i faked for each line and i didnt write down notes for that so lol. even without those, gallerian’s lines are enough to figure out what Nemesis is saying.　(Return to text)

  1. Was largely referring to this document and other resources to find the right legal jargon that would fit all the court-related stuff going on in here　(Return to text)

  1. yes i deliberately translated all mentions of his wife as “the wife”. their relationship isnt exactly the epitome of love anyway so i thought it would be hilarious for him to say that　(Return to text)

  1. Michelle did actually die, and was never brought back (tldr Ma lied). The “Michelle” Gallerian talks to in this part is actually the Clockworker’s Doll (Doll of Sloth itself) and off my head, Gallerian was fully convinced that the doll was Michelle. In his lifetime, he never did figure out the doll’s role as a Vessel of Sin, and this shows in his later lines in his final moments.　(Return to text)

  1. In this line he actually says the same thing as 5 lines later (何か言いたげだ lit. you look like you got something to say). Since Master of the Hellish Yard was speaking before this line, it didn’t make sense to just say that again, so I reworded this somewhat liberally.　(Return to text)

  1. This friend and the other friend are actually Hel Jaako and Feng Li. You can read up more about them on the Evillious wiki, but basically they were mad that Gallerian declared the general (who’d indeed massacred a village and their friend) innocent. And more info on THAT incident can also be found on the wiki.　(Return to text)

  1. Used pricechecktranslations’s tl of the line here. the saying is less literally “anything can be bought with money”. well tbh i think most of my references on the evillious wiki probably owes credit to pricechecktranslations, but this in particular is just really good as a whole and i have no idea how else one could phrase it in the context of gallerian better. It’s just perfect tbh　(Return to text)
