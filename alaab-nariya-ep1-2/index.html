Sorry, But You Have to Give Up

Scarabia Dorm - Hallway

Kalim
I want Yuu, who’s from another world, to personally witness the Yasmina River Fireworks Festival.
Plus, I also wanna show you how amazing the Scalding Sands is!
Grim
Fnagh~~! I wanna go too, yanno! There’s gonna be lots of tasty food, right!?
Kalim
Yeah! Everything there’s delicious! We welcome you, too, Grim!
Jamil
Wait just a minute! Don’t just carry on with the conversation on your own! Are you sure you can make such decisions on a whim?
Kalim
Don’t be so stuffy, even if it’s just the spectator’s bleachers, it’ll somehow work out in the end, right?
Jamil
While it’s true that we can probably secure seats with our organiser privileges… But that’s not the main problem now!
Grim
Organiser? What’dya mean by that?
Jamil
(Sigh… It’ll drag the conversation out, but I guess I’ll explain everything if the end result is them giving up on joining us.)
The nobility in the Scalding Sands take turns organising this festival, so…
This year, the Asim family will be organising it.
Taking charge of the fireworks festival is both an enormous task with no room for mistakes, and an honour for those who do.
Kalim
I was told to go around greeting the guests, y’know?
Jamil
And I’m required to escort Kalim, so we have to return to our hometown.
…Which means we don’t have any time to look after the two of you.
Sorry, but you have to give up on joining us.
Grim
Fnaaaaggghh!! I wanna go! I wanna go!! I WANNA GO!!!
Jamil
(Ugh! And I’d spent so much time explaining myself too!)
(Does this mean that no matter how thoroughly I explain my logic, it’s all for naught with Grim…)
Kalim
Please, Jamil! I want them to know how splendid the Scalding Sands is!
Grim
Pleeeease, Jamil~
If you don’t let us come along with you, then we’ll wreck havoc in Scarabia while you two are away.
Jamil
…There’s no choice, then. Fine.
Grim
Yaaaay!!
Kalim
Oh, right! Let’s invite our other friends to come along, too! Partying in a large group gets the mood up better anyway!
Jamil
Hang on! The VIP seats are limited, so that’s impossible!
Kalim
Really? But I remember we still had some seats leftover-
Exactly how many did we have left, Jamil?
Jamil
Grr…!
…If I recall correctly, we should have six seats remaining.
Grim
Then, Yuu and I can come along, right!?
Kalim
Of course!
And then… we can invite four others to join us, right?
Alright, I’m gonna let Cater and Lilia from the Pop Music Club know!
Jamil
Sigh… Who’d have thought we’d end up with four unannounced guests…
Kalim
Are you not gonna invite anyone, Jamil?
Jamil
Yeah, I’m not.
Even if I did, I’ll not become their caretaker.
Kalim
Hmm…
Jamil
(I really don’t want any more people to be coming along, thank you very much…)
Kalim
Then, I’m gonna ask Cater and Lilia to invite one more person each!
Jamil
What did you just say!?
Kalim
Alllright! I’m gonna go look for them now!
Yuu, Grim! Follow me!
Jamil
W-Wait, Kalim! …Tch, off he goes…
So we wound up have six extra people following along in the end? Grrr… What a sticky situation it became…!
