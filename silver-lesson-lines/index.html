Silver - Lesson Lines

Posted on 13th Mar 2023 (Monday) Edited on 12th Dec 2023 (Tuesday) In Twisted Wonderland , Lesson Lines , Silver

Contains: History, Flight, Alchemy

Good lesson lines are sorted in the game according to the time of the day of the lesson (randomly chosen when you enter).
You have to observe the sunlight’s direction in History, the background in Flight, and the hourglass colour in Alchemy to tell the difference.

History

Good

Morning

Silver
Let's learn from the past.
It's just memorisation.
A war tactic? I want to memorise that.

Noon

Silver
Staying seated too long isn't good.
Studying is also a form of forging the spirit.
Lilia excels at History.

Afternoon

Silver
A sleep-inducing curse… Ugh…
I have intensive training for horse riding after school.
Fallen nations…have their own histories too.

Great

Silver
This one's easy.
I triumphed over the sandman!
Got through without a scolding from Dad.
Uh oh, I don't get it.1

Perfect

Silver
Exactly what I'd been taught.
One day, Lord Malleus will also appear in textbooks!
Dad taught me this.
Phew… It's like this, right?1

Special Perfect

Silver
Just participate as if it's any other class.
Stay focused now.
Don't get thrown into disarray.

Flight

Good

Morning

Silver
That's the right trajectory.
Straighten up.
I'm good at physical exercises.

Noon

Silver
Nngh… *gasp*!
This is similar to horse riding.
Have you done your warmups?

Afternoon

Silver
You'll bite your tongue.
I was about to doze off in the middle of flying.
Keep calm no matter what.

Great

Silver
I've got stamina.
Simple.
This much is to be expected.

Perfect

Silver
I want to fly faster!
Lilia doesn't need a broom for this.
Horses are fussier than this.

Special Perfect

Silver
I want to watch the headmaster's demonstration.
Looks like I'm being watched.
I'm not particularly anxious.

Alchemy

Good

Morning

Silver
Focus.
Calm down.
The recipe…is…
It reeks like rotten eggs…
Not a problem.

Noon

Silver
If only we could use swords to solve this.
There are sparks are flying out of the potion…!
I have no interest in gold.
There's no easy way out.
We should do as the professor says.

Afternoon

Silver
Take a short break.
Eternal life…
Do it steadily.
Mixing is all about practice.
This can also hone your concentration.

Great

Silver
It's a success!
I learnt this from my dad.
I can do this in my sleep.
This is…passable…
Just like the textbook says.

Perfect

Silver
It's a gem. Do you want it?
This is just like cooking.
You can do it if you try. Don't think too hard.
I want to show this to Lilia.
Let's aim for the next one.

Special Perfect

Silver
I feel relieved with the headmaster around.
Keep calm…like you normally would…
Inattentiveness is the root of failure.
I will bring no shame to Lord Malleus.
I want to take some deep breaths.
