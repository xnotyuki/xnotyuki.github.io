We’re Always Like This

Scalding Sands - Camel Bazaar

Trey
Do you have any other food recommendations, Jamil?

Jamil
Then, how about Silk City’s locally developed Silky Melon?
Jamil
It is an excellent fruit that is rarely exported and is characterised by its silk-like texture along with a refined sweetness.
In addition… Don’t you think it is a fitting food for all of us here?
Grim
Whaddya mean by that?
Jamil
Do you remember the kind-hearted youth mentioned in the fireworks festival’s folktale?
Poverty-stricken he may be, but he was said to have split a melon into half to share with his friend…
Grim
Oi. Why’s he even able to afford to eat a melon when he’s poor?
Fruits are luxury items, yanno! I would eat ‘em everyday if I could!
Jamil
Who knows? We don’t know the specifics since it’s a folktale. Don’t think too hard about it.
Grim
Hmmm…
Jamil
Over time, sharing and eating this melon is said to allow your relationships to be everlasting…
In no time, it became an indispensable lucky charm for this festival.
Grim
I see…
Hm? But why’d you say that it suits us?
Jamil
Because my workload would decrease if our group could stick together and move as one.
Especially you, Grim.
Cater
That melon sounds delish plus it even has standout post potential, so it’s a gonna be a sure hit ♪ It’s a must try for me!
Trey
Yeah, can’t just overlook that if it’s something that can only be eaten here, can we.
Grim
Me too! I wanna sink my fangs into it with all my bite!
Jamil
There is a fruit stall up ahead that only opens on the day of the festival. I recommend trying the Silky Melon there.
That stall adds homemade melon ice cream to top off a melon that is cut in half-…
Malleus
Ice cream? That’s nice, I would like to have some as well then.
Jamil
I’d expected you would be interested since you like ice creams, Malleus.
How about Yuu?1

Select one of the choices to reveal the next dialogue

Jamil
Got it. I vouch for its taste.
Malleus
Oh? You couldn’t possibly be unwilling to strengthen your bond with me…could you?
Jamil
I’ll purchase enough servings for all of us. The stall is close by.

Trey
Chomp!
Mmm, this is good. This is probably the first melon I’ve ever eaten that has such a rich flavour.
Malleus
Indeed. The ice cream topping is also perfect. One can tell that plenty of melon juice was used in its conception.
Cater
The garnishes are lovely too~ ♪
Najma
No matter what everyone says, it’s the specialty of Silk City after all!
Cater
Hey guys! Let’s take a pic of us eating this together!
Malleus
But for what reason? Has it not been a short while since we took the group photograph at Asim’s house?
Cater
But you see~! It’s said that eating this Silky Melon extends friendships, right?
It’ll be a #SilkyMelon #proofoffriendship #friends4eva kinda snap, yanno?
Malleus
…I shall abstain.
Jamil
I’ll pass.
Trey
Me too, I don’t really like that sorta thing anyway.
Grim
More importantly, we gotta eat up all the ice cream before it melts right now! Slurpslurpslurp slurpslurp!
Cater
Denied by everyone except Yuu-yuu! Gosh, y’all are so defiant~
Najma
How strange. It’s just a photo, right? No harm in taking one, is there?

Psst, Jamil. Is everyone squabbling with each other or something?

Jamil

No such thing. We’re always like this.

Najma

Night Raven College sure is a weird school…

Cater
It’s still too soon for the Silky Melon’s blessings to take effect I guess~
