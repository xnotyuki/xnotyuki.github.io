Interactions with Deuce Tsum I

Main Street

(Deuce Tsum running)

Ace
Huff, huff… It went that way!
Deuce
…Now!

Flash

Deuce
Finally caught you…! Seriously, fleeing our dorm without warning? What are you thinking?

*Tsum reaction*

Deuce
Don't you struggle now! I'm absolutely not gonna let go.
Ace
Ughh, I'm beat. If the dorm head hadn't ordered me to help you catch the Tsum, I would've tapped out mid-chase for sure.
Seriously man. We have the Unbirthday party prep to do and you're already adding more to my plate?
Deuce
All you do is whine whenever you open your mouth, don't you? I can do the bare minimum of catching a Tsum on my own just fine even without your help, thank you very much.

*Tsum reaction*

Ace
Pfft! You two really are alike.
Especially with how you keep pushing people around and make that hateful expression!
Deuce
Don't describe me like I'm some sort of brat!
Ace
Yeah, yeah. Anyway, I'm famished from running so much this early in the morning~ Let's go and grab a quick bite at the cafeteria.
Deuce
…Right. You better behave yourself at the cafeteria, Tsum.

*Tsum reaction*

Cafeteria

Deuce
It's way more packed than I thought it'd be for a morning. Are those the Magift club members over there?
Ace
Ahh, you're right. They must've ended morning prac or something.
Savanaclaw Student A
Oi, look at that. Isn't that one of those Tsum things?
Savanaclaw Student B
The current talk of the school? Pupu! That stupid face it's got looks just like that guy with the spade suit on his mug.
Deuce
Say what…?
Ace
Stop reacting to every little thing from people who clearly have way too much time on their hands already. Don't pile on even more trouble for us.
Deuce
I…I know as much. The "stupid face" remark just baited me a little, that's all.
If we stir up any trouble, we'll get our heads lopped off by the dorm leader. Even I'm not foolish enough to want that.
Savanaclaw Student A
Oi, outta the way. This is where we always sit.
Savanaclaw Student B
Isn't it too early in the morning for you guys to be awake? Why don'tcha huddle up in the corner with your Tsum until sleepy time's over? Hahaha!
Deuce

Kuh… Spouting whatever they want without a care just because I'm keeping quiet…! It's not like where we choose to eat matters, does it?

Expand…!

Ace
…Hm? Wait a minute, isn't your Tsum getting bigger and bigger?!
It was sitting on your shoulder up till now, right?!

*Growing Tsum*

Deuce
Ah! Oi, Tsum! Where are you going?!
Savanaclaw Student B
Whoa?! Wh-What do you want?!

*Smack!*

Ace
Oh! That Tsum's got moves. It tackled the Magift club guy to bring him down.

*Bash! Bash!*

Savanaclaw Student B
Ow ow ow ow ow!! The back of my head smacks the floor whenever the Tsum headbutts me!
Deuce
O-Oi, Tsum, what in the world are you doing?! Stop causing trouble!
I gotta pull it away from his face first, otherwise…!

Stretch…!

Savanaclaw Student B
OW OW OW OW YEOWCH!! M-My hair!!
Savanaclaw Student A
Oi, stop! Don't just rip his hair off along with the Tsum!
Deuce
It's not like I can just let it carry on beating his head in either, can I?! Curses, it's stronger than I thought…!
If it comes down to this, then… Oi, Tsum! I'm gonna drop a cauldron on you if you don't back away from his head, got it?!

*Bash! Bash!*

Deuce
I'll take that as a yes then! Here it comes!

*Woosh!*

*CRASH!!*

Savanaclaw Student B

UGH!!

Deuce
Ahh! The Tsum evaded it! Sorry, I didn't mean to drop it on your head at all, I really didn't…!
Ace
Pfft! Heehee… What's this, a comedy skit…? HAHAHA!
Ace
Ahh, that was such a good laugh. That lookalike Tsum of yours ain't too shabby.
Deuce
This is clearly no laughing matter… Hopefully the dorm leader doesn't catch wind of the ruckus from earlier…
Look Tsum, even if you're feeling real annoyed, you gotta at least bear with it at that level. Even I can find a way to endure it somehow, you know.

*Tsum reaction*

Ace
Nah man it's funny anyway, so it's fine, y'know? The way it snaps at that level of taunting is exactly like you too, don't you think?
Deuce
Stop finding this funny! Acting as if you're a bystander to all this… You've got to be kidding me…
