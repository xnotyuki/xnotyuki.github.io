Interactions with Kalim Tsum I

Scarabia Dorm - Lounge

Kalim
Jamil! Let's bring the Tsum to campus and play now!

*Tsum happy*

Jamil
…Let's just begin with your reasons first.
Kalim
So earlier in my room, we actually…

Scarabia Dorm - Kalim's Room

Kalim
Say, Tsum. Aren't you getting tired of playing in Scarabia by now?

*Tsum react*

Kalim
True~ It's a little cramped, yeah. It'd be great if you could play somewhere more spacious.
…Right! Wanna explore the school campus, Tsum?
It's way bigger than here, and it has tons of fun stuff too!

*Tsum happy*

Kalim
Alright! It's decided then! Since this is such a special occasion, let's go invite Jamil to join us too!
Kalim
…So that was what happened.
Jamil
Isn't it just an impulse of yours, Kalim?
Anyway, it's going to disappear by tonight regardless, right? I feel that letting it remain in the dorm works perfectly fine.
Kalim
That may be true, but I wanna let it see all sorts of stuff around the whole campus since this is a such a special time for it!
Plus, even if something does happen, it'd all go down fine with you around, Jamil. Don'tcha agree?
Jamil
…Sigh. Okay, fine, I'll accompany you two. I implore you, don't you dare stir up any trouble, got it?
Kalim
Of course I do! Isn't that great, Tsum!

*Super happy Tsum*

Kalim
Since that's decided, let's go this instant!
Jamil
……I'm apprehensive.

Mister S's Mystery Shop

Kalim
So this is the school store. It sells all sorts of stuff, and Sam, the owner, is a great guy too!
Oh, is there anything you want? Go ahead, pick out whatever you like! Today's a special day anyway!

*Tsum choosing things*

Kalim
Ahkay, leave it to me!
Sam! Get us everything that's on all the shelves between here and there!
Sam
All right! Thanks for the business as usual, Kalim!
Jamil
Oi, Tsum. Don't you get carried away. And Kalim, don't just agree to whatever it says!

Main Street

Kalim
This is the school's main street. This was where the Tsum and I bumped into each other.

Kalim
Oh, are you interested in the Sorcerer of the Sands' statue? I get you, he's really cool, isn't he?
If you entered Night Raven College too, you'd probably make a good Scarabia resident!

*Happy Tsum*

Jamil
Tsums can't be admitted, can they… No wait, there are exceptions like Grim here too so, it's not impossible after all…?

Woods Behind Campus

?!

Kalim
Surprised? The trees are huge, aren't they!
There are lots of apple trees planted around here, so there are even animals that come here to grab a bite.
Sometimes I'd ride the magic carpet all the way here… I'd get real happy whenever I pick the apples and pass 'em to the animals that can't fly.

*Chirpy chirp…*

?!

*Distressed Tsum movements*

Kalim
Ahaha! The bird's pecking at you with its beak for fun. Looks like it's already taken a liking to you.
Jamil
…To me, it looks like the bird mistook the Tsum for a fruit and tried to eat it though.

Interior Hallway

Kalim
Now then~ Where should we go to next… Tsum, is there any place you wanna go?
Jamil
…Because we really can't make it to every spot in school by the end of the day, after all. We have two or three hours at most before we return to the dorm.
Kalim
Wha-, already?! We are ages away from being done showing it around!
Hmmmmm, two or three hours to go, huh… Ah! If we go now, then that place would be alright!
Jamil
"That place"? Where are you thinking of going?
Kalim
That's a surprise for later. Come on, let's go!
