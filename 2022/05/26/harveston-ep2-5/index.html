Apple Boa

Harveston - Epel's Home

Idia
Idia has equipped the Harveston Tunic! Jing-a-ling ♪ Cold resistance increased by 60%!
Deuhehe… Never would've thought I'd get the chance to wear one of these traditional outfits. Sir Epel's quite the thoughtful one!
Sebek
He sure is excited. I never would've expected this from Idia based on his usual behaviour.
Jade
Agreed. I was so certain he would have absolutely no interest in traditional wear of this sort.
Idia
Fuhee… That's because these are the same outfits worn by the heroines of Love Sled!
Sebek
Thought as much.
Idia
And the blue of this outfit in particular! It's the same colour that represents my best girl, Ai, and…
Sebek
You can stop right there with the animation talk.
Epel
Sorry to keep you all waiting!
Jade
Done changing, Epel? Your outfit seems to be quite different from the rest of ours.
It looks very puffy and fluffy… Like the embodiment of the very clouds themselves.
Epel
Th-Thank you for your kind words. You're kind of embarrassing me a bit, though…I guess.
The clothes I'm wearing now were sewn for me by my grandma, actually.
In fact, all your outfits were hand-made by all the villagers.
Jade
Is that so? Their workmanship is absolutely gorgeous.
Sebek
It's both warm and practical too.
Epel
We have a custom of knitting winterwear whenever the snowfall gets too heavy for farmwork to be done.
The designs and patterns differ from person to person, so we even hold exhibits to show off what we've knitted every year.
Jade
I see. Such is a way to spend time indoors productively even if one is unable to go out to work.

Select one of the choices to reveal the next dialogue

Jade
Good work. Do join us over here.
Sebek
Don't worry about it. I've also just finished changing anyway.
Sebek
Looks like your clothes fit nicely, Yuu.
Jade
Indeed. The adorable apple and flower patterns suit you very well.
Sebek
The villagers we saw along Harvest Highway weren't wearing these outfits, were they?
Epel
Right. They're tough to make and maintain, so nowadays, it's only really the Kelkkarotu participants that wear them.
That's been the case ever since ready-made clothes that are a lot easier to care for spread through the village. But the villagers do still love these outfits.
This traditional clothing is known as Apple Boa.
The name originally referred to the pom-pom on the hat that's shaped after an apple…
But over time, people began to refer to the entire outfit itself as Apple Boa instead.
Epel
Look, my hat has apple ornaments instead of a pom-pom on it, doesn't it?
Jade
It does indeed. It's incredibly adorable, actually.
Epel
Ador…?!
I mean, that's not so bad, either, but I was kinda hoping you would say something about how like…cool it is 'cause it's like I'm bearing my village's legacy on my shoulders or something…
Or even that it's cool because you can feel the enthusiasm everyone has for Kelkkarotu through it. Does it not give you that sort of impression?
Jade
Indeed, the outfit is very cool because of how it creates this sense of you bearing Harveston's legacy on your shoulders, while also getting across that enthusiasm you all have towards Kelkkarotu.
Epel
You're just parroting what I said.
Jade
Hehehe. The pom-poms on our hats are simply adorable as well. I've taken quite the liking to them.
Epel
Oh yeah, back when I was a kid, I accidentally pulled off one of those pom-poms because I kept messing with it.
And then Nannan…Grandma got so mad at me.
Jade
The nice texture does tempt one to continue touching it.
Sebek
Such frivolous decoration will only be a hassle when doing vigorous exercise. I think it would be perfectly fine to remove.
Epel
The pom-pom was originally meant to protect the top of one's head, actually.
Sebek
What…? Well, that certainly changes matters.
If that's the case, then it'd also be a good idea to attach pom-poms to my official escort uniform.
Jade
That would…make quite the fancy appearance.
I have become more fond of the gorgeous stitchwork than the warmth the clothing provides.
The snowflake and apple design certainly feels very Harveston.
In addition, the brooches we have on our chests are splendid in their own charming way.
Epel
The first things that come to mind when you bring up Harveston are apples and snow, after all. The brooches are also designed after them.
Sebek
Speaking of, I don't see Grim anywhere. Where did that rascal go?
Jade
I believe he is still in Epel's house. What could he be doing in there, I wonder?
Epel
Ahaha! He's probably crying tears of joy right about now.
Sebek
Hm? What do you mean?
Grim

Kept y'all waiting long enough!

Grim
Your greatest majesty has arrived, dressed in a brand new ribbon!
Idia, Jade & Sebek

OOOOHH!

Grim
C'mon, c'mon, have a good look! Ain't it cool?!
Epel
Yeah, it looks super good on you!
Idia
Yours truly is actually the kind who thinks that making pets wear clothes is a heresy…but that ribbon looks oh so good on you!
Grim
Did I just hear you say "pet"?!

Grim
Wahahaha! Of course it does!
Grim
Cute? Don't you mean cool?!
Epel
The elderly of the village also made that ribbon. Custom-made especially for you, Grim!
It has a snowflake and apple design to it, as well.
Grim
I've definitely taken a liking to it!
