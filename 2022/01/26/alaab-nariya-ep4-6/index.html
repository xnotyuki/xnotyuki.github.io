Stretching So Far!

Scalding Sands - Zahab Market

Cater
Hm? There’s a huge crowd at that ice cream store over there.
Trey
They look more like spectators than queueing customers. Maybe there’s something special about the ice cream?
Malleus
Ice cream of the Scalding Sands? I simply cannot overlook this opportunity to try some.

*Malleus walks over*

Malleus
May I have one, please?
Stallholder
Sure thing! Young man, you’re a tourist, right? You’re gonna be amazed the moment you bite into our ice cream.
I’ll prepare it right now, so hold this cup and wait for a bit.

*Scoop, scoop! Rattle rattle!*

Cater
Wow! The ice cream’s stretching so far after being churned with the paddle! So interesting~!

*Snap!*

Stallholder
And, done! Now, hold your cup out, young man.
Malleus
Alright.

*Boing! Woosh!*

Malleus
Muh-?!
Cater
Whoa! The ice cream disappeared right before his eyes…?!
Stallholder
C’mon now, if you can’t grab it properly it’d be trouble for me y’know.
Malleus
Alright. I will retrieve it without fail this time.

*Boing! Woosh!*

Malleus
……
Stallholder
Ahaha, how was that! Must’ve been your first time seeing the likes of stretchy ice cream, right!
Trey
Malleus… He’s gotten his ice cream pulled from right under his nose so many times. He’s definitely being kept in suspense…
Jamil

AAAAAHHHHH!!

I sincerely apologise for my fellow countryman’s insolence, Malleus! Even if it was in the name of fun, such a display of graceless behaviour was-!!
Pl-Please spare a thought to overlook this…!
Malleus
…What are you talking about, Viper?
Jamil
I mean… Um, you were furious about being tricked so that you could not retrieve your ice cream the entire time, right?
Malleus
Why would I get angry over such a trivial matter? I was impressed by the shop owner’s performance, in fact.
Stallholder
And the show ends here. Go on, eat up!
Malleus
Thank you. I shall enjoy it now.

*Bite…*

Malleus
This is quite the delicious flavour along with an interesting texture. I am pleased with this.
Jamil
Phew… That’s great…
Trey
Excuse me, I’d like to have one of these.
Stallholder
Okay! Thank you very much!
Kalim
You bought a bandanna, Trey?
Trey
Yeah, I’ve been keen on getting one ever since I put on these clothes.
It’s soft and beautiful… And I thought it might make for a perfect memento for today.
Kalim
It is said that textiles from Silk City are the best of all in the Scalding Sands~
Trey
I have pretty much no idea what to use this sort of thing for even after buying it. though.
Kalim
What to use it for? Isn’t it fine to just use it however you like?
Trey
Yeah, but… There might be some hurdles I need to overcome if I want to use this regularly.
Kalim
Really? The one you’re wearing now suits you really well too, yanno!
You can drop by Scarabia anytime if you’re unsure how to wear it.
Trey
That’d be a lifesaver.
Kalim

Because Jamil will teach you how!

Trey

You’ve never worn yours on your own?!

…I feel guilty for potentially bothering Jamil, so I’ll make sure to memorise the exact steps on how to wear it instead.
