…Pass It to Me

Scalding Sands - Camel Bazaar

Jamil
Hello, may I have some coconut juice, please?
Stallholder
Ohh if it isn’t little Jamil! It’s been a while, eh? Festival prep’s coming together well, yathink?
Jamil
Yeah, I guess so.
Stallholder
I’m looking forward to what incredible sights the Asims have to show!
Jamil
I see, I’ll be sure to inform them of your kind words.
Stallholder
So, how many are you looking to get today?
The usual 50? Or perhaps today’s a 100 day?
Trey, Grim and Cater

…So many?!

Jamil
No, I’d just like seven for today.
Stallholder
Oh, so you’re not shopping for Young Master Kalim now. Gotcha, I’ll get them ready in a jiffy.
Cater
…Jamil! Do you seriously always buy that much coconut juice?!
Jamil
That’s because it is Kalim’s favourite. When I buy more than usual, they are delivered via truck instead.
Trey
Even if it’s his favourite, there has to be a limit to how many he drinks at a time, right?
Stallholder
Right, sorry for the wait. Fresh coconut juice coming right up!
Malleus
…The coconut is served whole, but from where does its juice originate?
Jamil
A hole is carved out of the top of the fruit and you drink the juice inside the coconut from there.
Cater
How interesting it is to use a fruit itself to serve as a cup ♪
Najma
Here are the straws. This way, you can drink while holding the coconut.
Grim
Ohhh! Gimme, gimme!
Cater
Man, it’s so refreshing and tasty. And since it’s not too sweet, I can gulp it dry no problem~
Trey
Yeah, now I can get why Kalim would want to drink so much of it. …Although, 100 is still too much.
Malleus
Lilia had once told me of a juice that can be drunk by sticking a straw directly into the fruit. I see now that this was what he had been referring to.
I had been so certain that he was just pulling my leg at the time.
Jamil
In addition to its juice, you can also consume the flesh inside a coconut.
Grim
Alright, time to gobble this up whole!

*CHOMP!*

Grim
I-It’s too hard… My fangs were about to snap yanno!
Jamil
Who’d bite into the fruit like that?! You’re supposed to reach the flesh by slicing the fruit open with a knife first.
The coconut fruit is very tough. It’s simply unthinkable to even attempt to split it open without using tools.
Trey
If that’s the case, shall we borrow a knife from the stall?
Malleus
…Pass it to me for a moment, Clover.
Trey
Hm? Sure, but…
Malleus
All we need is to have this split open, correct?

*RIP!!*

Malleus
Here.
Trey
…Th-Thanks.
Cater
To be able to split a coconut into perfect halves like an egg… That’s yaboi Malleus for you.

*Bite*

Trey
So this is what coconut meat tastes like. It’s got a unique texture that reminds me of hardened yoghurt.
I’ve used processed coconut meat before to make sweets, but this is my first time having it fresh.
Najma
That’s so amazing! Please break mine open as well!
Jamil
H-Hey! Najma! Who do you think Malleus is?!
Malleus
I don’t particularly mind, actually.
Grim
Malleus! Open mine for me too!
Better ask him too, Yuu!
Cater
Then, mine’s up next~ ♪
Trey
Even Yuu and Cater…
Jamil
Malleus ended up becoming a coconut breaker… This is just…
Trey
I don’t think anyone back at school would believe us even if we told them personally.
