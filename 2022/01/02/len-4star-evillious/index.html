Riliane's Room

Riliane
Allen, what will we be having for teatime today?
Allen
…You will be having brioche, Your Royal Highness.
……
(The girl whom Riliane wished to have disappear is no longer here.)
(But the war with the neighbouring Kingdom of Elphegort continues still…)
(In the first place, Prince Kyle of the Kingdom of Marlon was the one who, despite having Riliane as his fiancée, declared he'd fallen in love and called off the engagement one-sidedly without a care.
Allen
(Thus, the Kingdom of Marlon and Prince Kyle should be to blame for this.)
(But Riliane loves Prince Kyle, so…)
(She ended up directing her rage not towards the prince, but that girl who is his lover, along with the Kingdom of Elphegort in which she resided.)
(Even though Elphegort and its subjects did no wrong…)
(Women from that kingdom who are innocent of any crime are probably being slaughtered even in this moment…)
Sorceress of the Three Heroes
I'll let you in on something as a bonus.
This kingdom… Lucifenia will soon fall to ruin.
Allen
Even then, I… will protect this kingdom and Riliane!
Allen
(In any case, to avoid that predicted ruin as well as to protect Riliane, this war must end.)
…Your Royal Highness.***
Riliane
Hm? What is it?
Allen
Why does the war continue without an end in sight still?
The lady who had robbed you of your fiancé has…
(The one who'd been my first love has…)
…already left this world. Your goal should have been accomplished.
Riliane
……
You do understand what it means to raise your comments to us, correct?
Allen
Yes. I'd wished to ask based on such an understanding.
Riliane
…As you've said, our goal is, indeed, accomplished.
However, our wants for that lush green kingdom occupy our minds ever so often.
Allen
So that's the reason once again? You've always been this way.
Wanting snacks, wanting the moon- And on the flipside, you dispose of whatever you dislike in an instant.
(I've always obeyed her, because I thought it was tied to her happiness.)
(That's why I killed my father, and why my first love…)
(But… At this rate, it'll be hopeless. If it stays this way, both this kingdom and you will…)
Allen
Your Royal Highness, please withdraw the troops from the neighbouring kingdom. If you are unable to comply, then-
Riliane
Oh? And what would you do then?
Allen
-I will depart from this palace.
Riliane
Wh…Wha-?!
What are you spouting, Allen?! We will not tolerate such insolence!!
In the first place, what's with your protests earlier! Is that how you speak to your master?!
Allen
-I was thrown all sorts of disparaging remarks, but Riliane agreed to my plea on the basis that it was special.
To go as far as to accept my views, it meant that Riliane intended to keep me by her side.
I was deemed a necessity.
That made me so, so truly happy…
With this, the war will end as well. Surely this would save Riliane from a destiny of ruin.
Allen
That was the mistake I'd believed in.
By then, it was already too late to do anything to force the hand of fate.
