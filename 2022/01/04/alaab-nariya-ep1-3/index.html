Sign of a Good Start to Our Journey

Classroom

Kalim
…And that’s why, I wanna invite both Cater and Lilia to the Scalding Sands!
Grim
Sounds like it’s gonna be an amaaazing festival, yanno!
Cater
Yahoo! Of course I'm gonna go, Kalim!
Never-before-seen foods in the charming streets… A palace rising from the depths of the desert…
No matter where you look, everywhere’s just super Magicammable!
I’ve been curious about the Scalding Sands since a while back too~
I gave up trying since I don’t have the money to even go overseas, but who would’ve thought I’d have a chance to go like this!
Cater
Plus, it’s the one day of the year when fireworks bloom all across the festival night sky! I can score a viral photo for suresies ♪
And to think I can view it from the best possible place! Truly blessed are those who are close to the Asims ☆
Kalim
Glad to make you happy too! What about you, Lilia?
Lilia
The Scalding Sands, is it? I visited it a long time ago. ‘Twas an interesting country.
I believe it should’ve changed a lot from back then. I’m interested to see how it has developed.
Cater
When’d you last visit the Scalding Sands for you to say "a long time ago", Lilsy?
Lilia
Kekeke… Unfortunately, I can’t tell you that. Keeping at least one tiny little secret gives me points for sweetness, don’t you agree?
Okay. Since Kalim was so kind as to invite me, I’ll go on this trip for some brief entertainment.
Kalim
So Lilia’s joining too! Woohoo!
I never would've thought that we Pop Music Club members could travel together. I’m super hyped!
Oh, right! We actually have two seats left to fill.
So you can each invite one of your friends to come along!
Cater
For real!? I wonder who I should ask~?
Lilia
Hrm… I’ve already decided.
I have the perfect candidate in mind. This will be a good opportunity for him to learn about a foreign society firsthand.
Grim
Learn about a foreign society? What d’you mean by that?
Lilia
Kekeke… That’s a surprise for tomorrow.

Exterior Hallway

The next day

Kalim
At last, it’s the day of the Yasmina River Fireworks Festival! Hurry, Jamil, let’s go to the Mirror Chamber!
Jamil
Don’t rush, there’s still a lot of time before our agreed meeting time with Yuu and the rest.
When we get back, you’ll have an important responsibility to fulfill, so you need to cut down on your merrymaking…
Kalim
I know, I know. I’m the type who makes sure to complete something without a hitch once I’ve put my mind to something.
Oh yeah, Grim and Yuu probably won’t oversleep, right?
I’m worried for them since our meeting time is earlier than our usual classes.
Jamil
There’ll be no issue with that. I told Yuu firmly to ensure Grim wouldn’t be late at all costs.
And just to be sure, I also passed five alarm clocks to them.
Jamil
Look, Yuu and Grim are on the other end of the hall, heading our way.
Jamil
I’m glad we could meet on our way to the Mirror Chamber. It’s a sign of a good start to our journey.
Grim
We came just on time!
I was so excited for the festival that I woke up before the alarm clocks rang!
But then, they kept ringing non-stop one after another… It was so noisy I couldn’t stand it, Jamil!
Jamil
Really? Seems like the task I entrusted Yuu with was excessive, if not harmful.
Grim
Apologise to me this instant!!
Kalim
I understand that feeling of waking up too early, Grim!
I was so hyped last night that I couldn’t get a good night’s sleep either!
Jamil was also staying up pretty late last night. Was it because you were too excited to sleep?
Jamil
Of course not. I was making preparations for today.
I checked our luggage, readjusted our schedule, and contacted the persons-in-charge… All while you were asleep.
Kalim
Whaaat~ If you’d told me I could’ve helped out too!
Jamil
……Sigh.
Forget it. That’s my responsibility anyway.
Kalim
You’re truly reliable, Jamil!
