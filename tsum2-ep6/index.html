Interactions with Lilia Tsum I

Diasomnia Dorm - Lounge

Sebek
Who could've expected the Tsums to appear on campus yet again…
Silver
Agreed. But no Tsums resembling Diasomnia residents have appeared this time, right?
Sebek
Sounds right. No ruckus in particular is happening in the dorm, and there's been no mention of them appearing eith-

*Tsum Lilia appears*

Sebek

!!! Wh-What the?! Something fell onto my shoulder?!

Sebek

You little…… You're a Tsum!!!!!!

*Happy Tsum*

???
Kehehehehe…
Silver
Ah, this laughter is…
Lilia
Yaaaay! Operation: Scare Sebek Silly was a great success!
Silver & Sebek

Dad! / Master Lilia!

Silver
I did realise that I hadn't seen Dad ever since the Tsums fell out of the sky at Ramshackle Dorm…
But I get it now. You were outside our dorm with your Tsum all along.
Lilia
Yep! What do you two think of this Tsum? Very very cute just like me, right?
Now Tsum, where are your manners? Go on and greet Silver and Sebek.

*Tsum greeting*

Silver
It feels strange since it looks like a shrunken-down Dad, but it's certainly adorable.
My name is Silver. Pleased to meet you, Tsum.

*Tsum greeting*

Sebek
I am Sebek Zigvolt. Although, it's not like you need to remember that anyway. More importantly, there's something I need to tell you.

*Tsum greeting*

Sebek
You… I mean… Sir?
Whatever the case, don't you dare be a nuisance to Master Lilia… I mean, please refrain from bothering him, got it???
Lilia
What's wrong, Sebek? You're speaking rather strangely now.
Silver
He's probably feeling perplexed about how to treat the Tsum since it looks like you, Dad.
Sebek
Sh-Shut up, Silver! Don't bring up superfluous things!
Lilia
Even though you were so relentless when talking to your own Tsum the last time? You're such a hilarious person, you know that?
Silver
…Hm? Dad, the Tsum disappeared from your shoulder. Where could it have…
Lilia
Ahh, about that-

*Tsum falls*

Sebek

!! You little… I mean, you, Sir?! Again?!

Stop messing… Please stop playing on my head?

*Happy Tsum*

Silver
The Tsum seems to be bouncing with happiness. Is it delighted that it managed to spook Sebek?
Lilia
Yeah. This Tsum seems to enjoy surprising people very much, you see.
And it appears quite fond of Sebek's surprised reaction.
Well then, now that I'm done introducing this little one to my two disciples…
It's about time I showed off this Tsum's loveliness to the other students too.
Now Tsum, onward! Let us spread the gift of bewilderment to everyone!

*Happy Tsum*

Silver
Dad, don't bother the other students too mu-… Hm?
Sebek
He disappeared in the blink of an eye…?! Why, that's Master Lilia for you!
Silver
…It'd be great if no commotion or the like happens on campus later, though.
Sebek
Hmph! What are you worrying for, Silver? You're not implying that Master Lilia would go out and cause problems on purpose, are you?
Although… It's not like I can say for certain that he absolutely doesn't either.
Silver
…Wanna go check on him afterwards just in case?
Sebek
…Let's.

Exterior Hallway

Lilia
See, this here is our school campus. Kehehe, looks like there's plenty of students frolicking about as usual.
What do you think? Here, you can relish in all the fun you want to your heart's content, don't you agree?

*Happy Tsum*

Lilia
I see, I see, delighted, aren't you? Getting out of the dorm was really the right call after all.
Now then… What will be your next move?

*Tsum look*

Lilia
…Oh? Did that student by the vending machine catch your eye?
Show me what you've got then, Tsum.

*Happy Tsum*
