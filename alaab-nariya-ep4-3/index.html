Refrain from Commenting Further

Scalding Sands - Zahab Market

Cater
Huh? Guys, Malleus isn’t here, y’know?
Kalim
…Ah! He just came out of the shop over there! He hasn’t managed to find a souvenir for Lilia, I guess?
Cater
Malleus has known Lilsy for a long time after all. He probably doesn’t wanna settle on a lacklustre gift~
Trey
I’ll have to make it a point to tell Lilia that Malleus picked out his souvenir in earnest when we return to school.
Jamil
Malleus. Is there anything you are troubled with?
Malleus
This is quite the difficult task as it is my first time purchasing a souvenir for someone.
I tried to search for a travel pennant since Lilia collects them from the places he visits…but it seems that there are none here.
Trey
Pennant…? I’ve never heard of that.
Malleus
It is a long triangular flag that is embroidered in different tourist attractions. It appears to be the standard souvenir item according to Lilia.
I’ve also received many pennants from him, and decorated my dormitory room with them.
I had thought that it would be the perfect souvenir considering this country has a flourishing textile industry, and yet…
Stallholder
It was a standard souvenir way, way back, but not now. I don’t think they’re in stock anymore.
Malleus
That is a shame…
Jamil
Textile… What about this, then?
Malleus
That shirt has quite a flashy design.
I’m also curious about its slogan which says “Gone are your restraints, don the freedom tee!”
Jamil
This is known as the Journey Shirt. It is said to be the shirt donned by the genie when he went on vacation.
To express his sheer exhilaration for his journey, he wore a shirt with both a flashy design and colour.
It was then developed as a souvenir for tourists based on the tale and the room it offered for unusual designs.
Malleus
The favourite shirt of the genie who loved travelling? This seems fitting for the expressive and cheerful Lilia.
I would like to purchase one of these Journey Shirts.
Stallholder
Thanks for your patronage!
You’re quite the lucky one, sir~ We actually have a Yasmina River Fireworks Festival Celebration Campaign ongoing now.
Stallholder
Buy one, get one free! I’ll prepare one for you as a gift. Here you go!
Malleus
What?
This colourful shirt…?
Jamil
Worn by Malleus…?
Trey and Cater
Gulp…
Malleus
……
…Shall I try it out next time? Perhaps this is where the true fun in travel lies.
Kalim, Jamil, Trey and Cater

EEEHHHH?!

Trey
Malleus? Wearing such a gaudy shirt…? No way, I just can’t imagine him in that at all!
Cater
Are you sure? You truly don’t think it’d look like you were forced into it for a dare?
Kalim
Sounds like fun so I think it’s fine! While you’re at it, why don’t you go to class next time while wearing the shirt?
Grim
…Y’all are making a huge ruckus when he’s just getting a souvenir for Lilia.
Cater
Are you looking for something, Trey? You’ve been walking to and fro the stores for a while now.
Trey
Yeah, I’m looking for tomato juice made from the finest ripe tomatoes as a gift for Lilia.
Cater
Lilsy does love tomato juice after all, yeah!
Malleus
I hear that the vegetables of the Scalding Sands are of good quality.1 I’m certain he would be pleased to know that as well.
Trey
It’s sold at lots of stalls back in Camel Bazaar, but I can’t find any here.
That’s my bad. If only I’d bought it while we were there.
…Can’t be helped then. Should I just give up on that and buy something else?
Malleus
What do you intend to seek?
Trey
Maybe…culinary spices?
Malleus
?!
Trey
Lilia cooks occasionally, doesn’t he? He often tells me in class about how he'd cook for Silver and Sebek2.
Cater
That might be good! He’d probably be delighted if you get seasonings that can be oft-used.
Trey
These red capsicum and dried mint seem to be the standard spices used in the Scalding Sands.
Olive oil and nut oil are pretty famous too. Plus, pomegranate molasses dressing3…
Malleus
…Wait.
Trey
What’s wrong, Malleus?
Malleus
Have the two of you ever eaten Lilia’s cooking?
Trey
I never did.
Cater
Me neither! You must’ve tried it before, right Malleus?
Malleus
Yes. Lilia’s cooking…has a flavour so unique that it cannot be expressed with words.
Trey
Basically, you mean…it doesn’t taste good?
Malleus
I’ll refrain from commenting further.
However…
If you do insist on giving seasonings to him, Clover…then you will need to brace yourself accordingly after.
The same applies to myself, and all of you here.
Trey
…G-Got it. If you’re going so far as to say that, then I won’t do it.
Sigh… Looks like I’ll have to settle with tomato juice in the end. I hope I can find it…

Notes

  1. I originally wanted to omit this line (熱砂の国の野菜は品質が良いと聞く) because in much of the earlier part of Episode 3 (after Najma appears iirc) there was a lot of dialogue about how nice the fruits, veggies and crops are super poggers here. It felt strange that Malleus would bring it up here as if he wasnt listening to the Viper word vomit　(Return to text)

  1. He actually says シルバーたち (Silver and the rest) but like it felt awkward to just say “yeah. silver and the rest. who? the rest. thats all”. I think Lilia does cook for the both of them at least. I don’t know if he cooks for the dorm or not.　(Return to text)

  1. Pomegranate molasses is a type of Persian seasoning which I thought would fit the Scalding Sands setting. I thought since these are supposed to be Scalding Sands stuff, keeping to the generic ザクロのドレッシング (pomegranate dressing) wouldn’t hit the mark.　(Return to text)
