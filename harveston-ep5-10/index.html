Notes

Aaaaand it’s finally done! Between this and Scalding Sands, I feel that the pacing is much better this time around. They really did learn from Scalding Sands (which to its credit, is still a good story, albeit has its kinks since it’s the first of its kind) and the ending is not a pathetic 2 parter, and feels more like a conclusion. I hope you all enjoyed this as well!
