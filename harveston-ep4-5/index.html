Totally Off From What I Thought!

Harveston - Outside Assembly Hall

Epel
Grandma, we're back!
Marja
Oh, finally back aren't ye, you rotten apples!!
You lot made sure to practise sledding properly before coming back, right?! Otherwise, the only dinner you're getting tonight is the one in your dreams!
Epel
O-Of course we did! Everyone improved so much that they've practically turned into totally new people!
We'll definitely get this win in the bag!

Marja
Oh-hoh… That look you've all got in your eyes now is exactly what I wanted to see. Hmm…
You dearies did your very best for practice after all. I'm oh so very proud of you all. You did wonderfully, loves.
Grim
Epel's granny went back to normal all of a sudden?!
Epel
Ahaha… She's a real nice person as long as Kelkkarotu isn't part of the conversation.
Marja
By the way, Epel. Have you all decided on your team name yet?
Epel
Ah! W-We forgot about it…
Marja

WHAT DID YE JUST SAY??!!!

DIDN'T I TELL YE THAT IT'S AS CRUCIAL AS PRUNING THE BRANCHES IN THE APPLE ORCHARDS???!!!
Epel
S-Sorry! We'll decide on it right now, so… U-Uhm…
What do we do, Yuu? You don't happen to have any good ideas, do you?

Epel
The most Harveston thing we have here would be apples, then.
Epel
I wonder what could be the symbol of the festival. Like the bonfire, the stalls, the food…
…And the apple lanterns strung up in the square.
Epel
Ah! Why don't we give ourselves a name based on the apple lantern hung on my sled?
Jade
I see where you're coming from. It is because the lantern will be our guiding light during the race, correct?
Epel
"The Apple Lanterns"… Yeah, it sounds great!
Idia
Hah? Are you kidding me? It doesn't sound like a good name to me at all.
Sebek
Agreed. It's not a bad name, but I think it'd be better if it could embody more power in it. How about "The Strong Apple Lanterns" instead?
Jade
I believe it would become an even more splendid name if it were "The Mountain-loving Strong Apple Lanterns".
Idia
Forget what I said, let's go with the original one!
Epel
Alright, it's decided then. Thanks, Yuu!
Grandma, our team name will be The Apple Lanterns!
Marja
I see, I see. I understand, dear. I will enter you boys under that team name, then.
Now, do you lads wish to stand out here in the cold all night? Hurry and make your way into the assembly hall, loves.
Epel
Yeah, gotcha.
…Huh? Sniff sniff… This smells like…?

Harveston - Assembly Room

Epel
Woohoo! It's barbequed meat!!!!
Grim
Plus there's loads of other amazingly delicious-looking food set out!
Marja
Why, this welcome party was prepared specially for you lovely lads.

Thank you very much, it is an honour.

Alrightie! I'm gonna go stuff myself with food tonight!

Cheers!
