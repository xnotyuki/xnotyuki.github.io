Just a Humble Attendant

Scalding Sands - Zahab Market

Monkey
Ukikiiii!
Thief
You’ve worked hard. How are the gains like?

*Jingle jangle!*

Thief
A bountiful harvest! Well done! Certainly befitting of the one fireworks festival in the year.
There’s so many people blissfully carried away by the festival. What good little sitting ducks they are!
And there’s still gonna be a lot more visitors too. Swipe left, right and center, and the profits come rolling in!
???
To command a monkey to pickpocket others from the shadows… That’s certainly a feat that can only be accomplished by a street performer.
However, your foolish charade ends now.
Thief
!! Who’s there?!
Jamil
Just a humble attendant who hates troublesome situations.
Thief

Wh-Why do you know about this place?!

Jamil
I just thought that you’d choose to hide in the most secluded area so that you could inspect your haul.
Thief
Tch! Like I’d let you catch me!
Jamil
Hah! It’s pointless even if you run.

Thief
Kuh! You had your gang with you?
Unfortunately for you though, this city is where we were born and raised, so escaping is simple. If I use this side path…

Thief

What?!

*More running*

Grim
You can’t pass here!
Jamil
You’re not the only one who knows the ins and outs of this city.
Try all you want. You won’t throw us off even if you intend to use the back entrance or escape with a boat on the canals.
All the paths leading from here have been blocked by remarkable mages.
So you better not think that you can get away.
Thief
…If it comes down to this, then all I’ve got left is to run with all my strength! I’ll make you suffer for this!
Jamil
Speak for yourself.
Escorting my seniors from school and being a tourist guide… All I’ve had was trouble after trouble to deal with over here!
You really think I’m gonna just sit back and let a pickpocket dunk all my efforts down the drain like this?!!
The price you’re paying for this…will be steep.

Scalding Sands - Silk City

Police Officer
There has been a succession of pickpocketing cases, so we’ve been searching for the culprit until you caught him.
Thank you, your efforts are a great help. Leave the rest to us.
Thief
Sob… I won’t ever steal again, so please forgive me…
Monkey
Ukiiii…
Police Officer
We’ll also return all the stolen goods to their respective owners.
Jamil
Excuse me, there is actually something that I was looking for, so I would like to just retrieve that first.
Sir, is this the USB flash drive that was stolen from you?
Pyrotechnician
Ohh, this is it! This is my USB drive!
You truly saved my life here!
Kalim
That’s great, old mister! Now the fireworks can be launched!
Pyrotechnician
Yeah, thanks to all of you, all of the cityfolk can enjoy the fireworks festival in high spirits.
Grim
Wahahaha! Wasn’t I amazin’?
Trey
Isn’t this thanks to Jamil who nabbed the thief instead of it being a stroke of Grim’s genius?
Jamil
That’s not the case at all. I was merely doing my job.
Now, it is almost sundown. Let us make our way to the event venue!
