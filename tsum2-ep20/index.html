The Tsums Return! II

Ramshackle Dorm

Rook
Ohh, the Tsums are rising up to the sky! What a gloriously beautiful sight to behold!
Kalim
I'm kinda reluctant to part ways so soon. We haven't played enough, y'know, not at all.
Jade
Certainly. It is such a shame for the Tsums to return this soon when they were the ones who blessed us with a bustling campus for the duration of their stay.
Azul
It falls within my expectations since the Tsums returned the day after they appeared the last time as well. More importantly, we have to think of what to do moving forward.
I'm sure that the Tsums will remain as the hot topic among our student peers for roughly a week more, so let us draft a new menu in the meantime.
If we made dishes and sweets modelled after the Tsums, we could possibly sustain our ability to draw in customers for a while.
Deuce
I couldn't focus on anything 'cause I was looking after the Tsum the whole time… I have to hurry back to the dorm and finish up the assignments that are due tomorrow.
Ortho
What kind of place is the Tsums' homeland, I wonder? Could I meet them there if I were to construct a teleportation device?
Lilia
Let me tag along too if you do manage to complete it. It could allow me to reach the lands I have yet to visit.
Kalim
Ooooi! Tsum! Come play with us again, okay!
We'll welcome you any day, anytime! I'll make sure to throw an even bigger banquet then, so look forward to it!

The End?
