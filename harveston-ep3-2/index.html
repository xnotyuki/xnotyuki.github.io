Wanted To Be “It”

Harveston - Assembly Room

Marja
Now then, let's start getting you ready to sled, shall we?
Pick your favourites out of this mountain of fabric, boys.
Jade & Sebek
…Huh?
Epel
We use this fabric to make the plushies that pull the sleds.
Sebek
Oh, right… The sleds we'll be racing in use plush toys to pull the sled, not dogs or horses.
Crimson red ones and checkered patterns… All these fabrics sure are gaudy.
Jade
And it appears that their sizes and lengths vary as well.
Epel
That's because these are scraps of cloth that the villagers have finished using.
Idia
…So what you're trying to say is that they're leftovers.
Epel
But it's not like we're trying to be cheap or anything, y'know!
We believe in cherishing what we have, so we reuse stuff by giving them a new purpose after they get worn down.
You'll be sewing the pieces together to make a patchwork, so pick out several pieces of fabric, okay?
Sebek
A-Are we really only limited to what this pile has to offer…?
……
But now that I think about it, any random cloth should suffice. They don't have anything to do with the sled's speed, after all.
Epel
They do, actually.
Sebek

SAY WHAT?!

Epel
The plushie's air resistance changes based on the surface material of the fabric, and water-repellent cloth won't get heavier from the snow.
They greatly impact the sled's speed and durability as a result.
Sebek
F-For real…?
Idia
Fuheehee! I'm already done choosing mine~
Sebek
That fast?!
Idia
Cuz I already researched the type of fabric used for the plushies that won in past races.
With these, I'll be able to make a high-performance plush for sure!
Epel
You researched that deep into Kelkkarotu?
Idia
Fuheehee… I'm a warrior who enjoys character creation, after all.
Sebek
…I'll use this fabric with snowflakes and this one with a dot pattern as a base, and add this orange one as an accent.
Jade
I see now. So this is the design of clothing you would pick for your own dailywear, am I correct, Sebek?
Sebek
That's not it! I was just referencing the fabric that Idia chose for himself!
Anyway, what about you, Jade? Have you not decided yet?
Jade
Oh, right. I must be swift with my choice… Please give me a moment.
…
……
………
…I've decided. I shall use this green and brown cloth as well as this piece with snowflakes and this other one with blue stripes.
Sebek
You sure took your time, huh.
Jade
The choices of cloth were all so wonderful that I could not firmly settle on any of them.
Idia
…That wasn't what the look on your face said at all.
Epel
I wonder which ones I should pick… Ah, this is-!
The same pattern as the clothes I wore as a kid! I loved them so much that I always wore them back then.
I'm gonna use this cloth then!
Jade
In your younger days, Epel? You must have been quite the lovely child, I'm sure.
Marja
Indeed, he was. He was such an energetic kid, playing outside all day.
Epel
Yeah. There weren't any kids around my age, so I'd play with the adults instead.
A game we played often was tag! Harveston has lots of places that are spacious and good for running around in.
But everyone kept trying to look out for me all because I was younger than them, and they wouldn't tag me.
I wanted to be "it" so bad that I would try to get myself tagged on purpose, but the others would run away from me in an effort not to tag me instead…
When that happened, I'd chase after the "it"s, I think?
Sebek
…What a bizarre game of tag.
Jade
You seem to have been doted upon by the villagers, Epel.
Epel
But when I started going to elementary school outside of Harveston,
I'd get into fights with my classmates really often.
Marja
Hehehe! He would often come home crying back then.
Epel
B-Because I was still little!
That did happen when I first started going to elementary school,
but I didn't wanna lose, so I kept fighting back against the older kids until I could finally get my revenge on everyone!
Sebek
Oh ho, what a thrilling tale that is.
Marja
He started being known as the Poison Apple of the Felmiers. Ahh, how nostalgic this all is.
Jade
Poison apple?
Epel
It means that I'm a formidable opponent despite my looks…apparently.
If they were gonna give me a nickname, they should've went for a better one like "Rising Dragon of Mount Moln" or "Wild Stallion of Harveston" or something.
Jade
…What outstanding nicknames.
Idia
Man… If Sir Epel ever gets into online games, he better discuss his display name choices with someone first.
