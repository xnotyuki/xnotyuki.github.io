Interactions with Jade Tsum I

Octavinelle Dorm - Jade's Room

*Close up of Jade Tsum*

Floyd
Ahahaha! It's a total splitting image of ya! This is the Tsum that Jade found?
Jade
Why, yes. Azul and I encountered it yesterday night.
…Speaking of which, thank you for assisting with checking on the status of the dorm for us, Floyd.
Floyd
It's no biggie. Was a disappointment for me, though.
'Cuz I was totally expecting someone's room to be busted beyond hope or something since ya mentioned "abnormal circumstances" and all.
Jade
What a Floyd-like response that is. Now, Tsum, shall we get going?

*Happy Tsum react*

Floyd
This early in the mornin'? So you're going up the mountains?
Jade
Why, we are just going to have a stroll on campus. I was thinking of taking the opportunity to strengthen my friendship with the dear Tsum while showing it around the campus facilities.
I am planning to enlist its help in the Mostro Lounge up until the Tsums are required to return, you see…
And when it comes to a colleague you work with, being on good terms with them is second to none.

*Happy Tsum jump*

Floyd
Hmmm. I'mma go back to sleep then.

*Floyd leaves*

Jade
Now then… Tsum. Once again, it is a pleasure to make your acquaintance.

*Happy Tsum react*

Botanical Garden - Temperate Zone

Jade
We have arrived at our destination. What are your thoughts, Tsum?

*Happy Tsum jumping*

Jade
Hehe, I'm glad that it is up to your liking. That is of utmost importance.
I'd thought that you would be attracted to sceneries reminiscent of the mountains like I am… After all, you do resemble me, Tsum.
In addition… I would love for you to have a look at this.

*Tsum look up*

Jade
These are the mushrooms that I am growing in the botanical garden here.
What do you think? With such a magnificent size and cap shape, they look quite delectable, don't you agree?
I endured many a hardship in order to raise it to such an exceptional size and beauty. There was the selection of its log, the pest control, the humidity regulation, and so much more…
Oh, right. Why don't you try one for yourself, Tsum? As a token of our budding friendship, I can even prepare a simple dish with…

*Tsum sniff*

*…Squash!*

Jade

Hah?

…Dear little Tsum, pray tell, what are you doing?

*Tsum look up*

Jade
I worked so hard to raise it to the exquisite state it is in and now it's all squashed! Ahh, how very pitiful it is…
???
…You sure are noisy while there's someone sleeping right here.
Jade
…Oh my, that voice is…
Leona
Ah? Oh, if it isn't Azul's Henchman 1.
And here I was beginning to wonder if a lost parakeet wandered in from somewhere 'cause of all yer grumbling.
Jade
So you were here as well, Leona. Please forgive me for the racket I'd caused.
But I must ask, why nap in this corner of the botanical gardens? I thought that you would prefer an area that gets plenty of sunlight instead.
Leona
'Cause the dorm guys are lookin' for me and I don't wanna get roped into troublesome stuff.
Jade
I see, so that is why you picked a different location from usual…
Leona
Exactly. And I'm taking a nap while killing some time. Take that armadillo with you and scram already.

*Tsum react*

Jade
By armadillo… You mean the Tsum, correct? I certainly can't deny that there is some resemblance between the two.
But that aside, please rest assured, for we do not intend to bother you in any way, Leona.
…Isn't that right, Tsum?

*Tsum jumps on Leona's shoulder*

Jade
Oh my, my, climbing onto Leona's shoulders… You truly are a sensible little Tsum, aren't you?
Leona
Oi… What are you doing? I'm pretty sure I told you two to get lost.
Jade
We are well aware, yes. We will be sure to leave as soon as the Tsum is done with its little massage.

*Crrck, crrack*

Leona
A massage, you say?
Jade
Correct. It is to allow you to have a restful slumber since you mentioned that you will be taking a nap afterwards.
Leona
Tch…! Don't kick up a racket like you did just now.
Jade
Fret not, for the dear Tsum is quite the discerning fellow. In addition…
The way its kneading technique effectively relieves you of your tension. Its execution is so stellar, highly-praised in Octavinelle even, that no one would believe it was all the work of a Tsum.
Leona
……
Jade

…Have a good night, Leona.

*Rustle…*

Jade

Going as far as to spread a sheet over Leona… Rather than admirable, I dare say you are truly a dependable Tsum.

…Could that be the protective tarp used for gardening? I find that using it as a blanket is rather, hmm…

Nevermind that. More importantly, to think that you would be so bold as to approach that Leona without a sliver of hesitation…

Accomplishing such a feat is not as easy as it seems.

Could your endearing form be useful in lowering one's guard, I wonder?

Jade

Oh Tsum… You are quite the daunting person… No, creature, aren't you?

*Happy Tsum*
