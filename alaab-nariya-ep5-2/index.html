This Year’s Fireworks are Especially…

Scalding Sands - Fireworks Bleachers

Jamil
It’s almost time for the fireworks to be launched.
Kalim
How was it? Silk City sure was a fun place, wasn’t it?
Cater
Yeah! It’s only been one day, but it’s been an impressive experience all around!
Malleus
I had not known that travelling was so free and fascinating.
I will be sure to thank Lilia as well when we return.
Grim
I’m so full from all the yummy food I got to eat! If this is what a vacation feels like, then I wanna be on a vacay everyday~
Trey
Getting to wear these traditional costumes is also a good memory I’ll cherish.

*Boom! BOOM!*

Kalim
Ah! The fireworks have started!
Cater
Wow! Amazing!
Trey
This is my first time seeing such huge fireworks!
Malleus
It is as if you could reach out and touch them with your bare hands.
Jamil
You know, it’s a waste to simply look up at the fireworks.
Trey
Hm? What do you mean by that?
Kalim
Merrily dancing under the fireworks is the Yasmina River Fireworks Festival way!
Jamil
The people broke into dance to celebrate the marriage of the princess and the youth in the folktale.
Why don’t we all dance in celebration of the couple since we have the opportunity right here?
Malleus
If it indeed is a tradition of this country, then allow me to join as well.
Grim
Dance with us too, Yuu!
Jamil
I’ll also need to prepare the special fireworks’ signal.
…Now then, I believe everyone is ready, yes?

*Whoosh~… KABOOM!!*

RHYTHMIC: Let’s Dance under the Fireworks!

Jamil
Huff… Huff…
How did it go? Did the signal go off on time? I didn’t mess it up, did I…
Visitor A
Amazing! They’re such huge fireworks!
Visitor B
The fireworks are being launched to the music’s beat.
This is my first time visiting such a romantic and dynamic fireworks festival!
Najma's Friend
The fireworks sure are lavish once again, aren’t they, Najma?
Najma
Yeah!
Plus somehow I feel like…this year’s fireworks are a step up from the usual!
Jamil
…Looks like everyone’s getting excited about it. I guess my job went without a hitch.
I’m glad… Truly…
Cater
Your timing was perfect, Jamil! It was the best oomph we could ask for ♪
Malleus
Yes. You were spectacular, Viper.
Trey
The momentous task sure did go swimmingly.
Kalim
This is the Yasmina River Fireworks Festival that we’ve seen since we were kids.
You must’ve been a little nervous about sending the signal, right? Were you alright?
Jamil
Hmph, of course I wasn’t.
This much is child’s play.
Trey
The night sky being blanketed by humongous fireworks was such a stunning sight to behold.
All those unique multi-coloured fireworks… They had such an impressive and ephemeral beauty.
Malleus
So those are the fireworks that Silk City takes pride in. Those wondrous rings of light are a sight befitting of the folktale.
I was surprised to the very end knowing they were designed not with magic, but with machinery.
Cater
I also managed to take the perfect video of the fireworks since I’ve set my smartphone camera to night mode, yanno!
I’ll send it to everyone afterwards, ‘kay. With this, we can remember today’s events anytime, anywhere.
Grim
This is my first time seeing such huuuuge fireworks! It was so amazing I’ve got chills running down my tail!
Kalim
It’s all thanks to Jamil that everyone could enjoy it so much!
Jamil
It’s not like that. However…
I do look forward to the look on Najma’s face when she finds out that I played such a critical role tonight.
This is my first time watching the Yasmina River’s fireworks with such a clear heart.
…It’s been an enjoyable festival.
Kalim
Yeah! It’s been so much fun!
Hey, Jamil, let’s watch the fireworks with everyone next year too!
Jamil
Hah?!
Grim
Ohh! You better invite us then, Jamil! I’ve taken quite a liking to this festival, yanno!
Trey
Yeah, it was quite fun. I’d be glad if you were to invite us over again.
Malleus
I do wish to know more about this country as well.
Cater
Welcoming that possibility with the most open of arms ♪ I did get to snap so many good photos today that I don’t even know which ones to upload first.
Kalim
Look, everyone’s saying so too. We’ll be counting on you, Jamil!
Jamil
I’m not kidding here… How hard do you think I had to work just for today?!

*Boom! BOOM!*

Kalim
Ah! The climax for the festival has finally begun! Loads of huge fireworks are being launched into the air!

Grim
Ooh! It’s so super pretty!
Malleus
Indeed. It is quite mesmerising…
Cater
Mmhmm, it’s so beautiful~
Trey
Yeah, it’s stunning.
Jamil
It truly is beautiful. This year’s fireworks are especially…
……
…Kalim.
Kalim
Hm?
Jamil
…Maybe watching them with everyone isn’t so bad after all.
