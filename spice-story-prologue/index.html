“Food”.
That is the foundation for life.
Kalim
The clear seas.
The great mountains.
The gentle earth.
We can sustain ourselves with whatever mother nature offers.
Trey
If you so wish, we will impart our wisdom and valor to you.
Kalim
Go forth!
Trey
Don’t turn back!
Kalim
The culinary road is harsh and unforgiving.
Trey
But I’m sure that when you reach the top, you will make it yours.
The glorious–
Trey & Kalim
Honour of being a master chef!

Cafeteria - Judging Venue

Crowley
The harsh and unforgiving culinary road… Countless hardships befall on those who attempt to reach its peak.
Crowley
Will the ruthless, yet impartial trials and tribulations leave you in despair or in bliss…
Crowley
…Right! Now that we’ve got that out of the way, are all of you prepared to participate in “Master Chef”?
I believe you all chose this program after carefully reading through the course syllabus, but let’s review what Master Chef is about once more.
Crowley
Eh… Ahem!
Crowley
“Master Chef” is a class that allows you to learn cooking techniques by helping out in the school cafeteria.
At present, you are able to eat delicious food in the cafeteria. However, once you leave your dorms here you will need to prepare your own meals.
Crowley
Eating out all the time will make your food expenses skyrocket, while eating only your favourite foods everyday will lead to malnutrition.
Crowley
If you destroy your heart and soul because of that… Becoming a great mage would be nothing more than a fever dream!
Let’s learn about nutrition and meal planning so that you can live long, healthy and independent lives.
To advance to the next year and eventually graduate from Night Raven College, you need credits from both your core subjects and your electives…
Crowley
You can gain one elective credit from Master Chef if your dish is well received by the judges.
Crowley
Captivate the judges’ stomachs and both your cooking skills and credit will be recognised!
Crowley
By the way, the judges are randomly selected from the students and staff in this school on the day the judging is held.
Why did we come up with this system? That’s because…
Crowley
If the judging panel is decided beforehand, student after student will make illicit deals with the judges to pass!
Crowley
Goodness, why is it always the underhanded methods that you work for… Oh, I’m getting off-topic.
Crowley
Right. There is also a special perk that is offered only in Master Chef.
Helping out the cafeteria’s kitchen is part of your lessons. In other words, you are being put to work. Therefore…
Crowley
Everyone here will be paid as part-time workers!
This means you will learn the importance of hard work as Master Chef students.
Isn’t it generous? No need to thank me, of course, for I am gracious after all!
Crowley
Now, I’ll introduce the ghost chefs who will personally instruct you for this program.
Head Chef
Nice to meet you~
Crowley
They were professionals who displayed their talents at the five-star restaurants they worked at in their former lives.
I’m sure you will grow as chefs under their tutelage!
At times, you will struggle as you adapt to the kitchen work, but I hope you will do your best to follow to the chefs’ instructions.
And last of all…
Everyone who’s participating in this round, please introduce yourselves and state your motivations for joining this program one by one.
Trey
Right. I’m Trey Clover from class 3-E.
I joined this program because I want to get better at guiding my fellow dorm mates in sweet making.
I want to understand how it’s like being a rookie starting with the lessons here.
Kalim
I guess next up is me. I’m Kalim Al-Asim, register number 1 in class 2-A!
Kalim
I joined because I also want to be able to prepare food for my guests whenever I host a banquet.
Kalim
I’ve never really cooked before, but if it’s fun then it should be fine!
Head Chef
We won’t go easy on you all, so make sure to work hard!
Kalim
Alright!
Trey
I’ll be in your care.
