Interactions with Lilia Tsum II

Exterior Hallway

Lilia
Show me what you've got then, Tsum.

*Happy Tsum*

*Gulp gulp*

Heartslabyul Student
Mmmm, man, this juice is really so darn good. It's such a waste for it to only be a limited-time release. I'm gonna enjoy it to the very last drop…

*Tsum lands on head*

Heartslabyul Student

PUUUI!!! COUGH! HACK!! S-Something fell on my head! I-I-I-Is it a bug?!

*Tsum runs*

Heartslabyul Student
Huh? But I don't see anything near me. That's weird, was that just my mind playing tricks on me…?
Lilia
Oh! So you have returned, Tsum. I had a verrrry good look at the way you scared that student while hidden away myself.
You landed on his head at the perfect moment when he still had juice in his mouth. On top of that, you immediately fled unseen…
Kehehe, very fine work that was! Let's keep this up and make our next move!

*Happy Tsum*

Library

Lilia
Yo! I say, such fine weather we have today.
Diasomnia Student
Ah, Senior Lilia. Right, it's pretty warm and pleasant…

*Lilia and Tsum switch places*

Diasomnia Student

HUH?!! Wh-Wha-? Something weird just appeared where Senior Lilia was standing?!

It looks like him, but it's smaller and there's something off about it… What's this? Magic? Did he transform?

*Tsum runs away*

Diasomnia Student

Wait, when did it- even the creature is gone too?! What in the world is happening?!?!?!

Interior Hallway

Lilia
Yay~ High five! Kehehe, our substitute surprise prank was a great success as well.
Alrighty, on to the next one!

*Happy Tsum*

Main Street

Silver
Father isn't returning to the dorm anytime soon. Just where did he go and what could he be up to with his Tsum…
Savanaclaw Student
Oi! Did you see Lilia Vanrouge and that thing that's like a mini version of him?!
Scarabia Student
I did. To be precise, they scared me so bad that I almost jumped out of my skin…
Savanaclaw Student
So you're also a victim of theirs, huh… Why don't we band together and take our revenge on him then?
Scarabia Student
Yeah! Let's go find them quick!
Silver
Father…
Sebek
Word of Master Lilia and his Tsum's activities have spread quite a fair bit, huh. That's Master Lilia for you!
Silver
I want to stop the hateful rumours surrounding Father from spreading further.
It'd probably be better for us to find Father and his Tsum before the uproar escalates.
Sebek, I'll check the outdoors, so go into the main building and…

…!!!

Sebek
What's wrong, Silver? Why did you hold your breath all of a sudden?
Silver
My back…is itching really bad for some reason…
Lilia

WAH!!!

Sebek
?! Master Lilia! When did you get behind us…!
Lilia
To think that you two would let us score a rear attack like this. Silver and Sebek, you two still have a long way to go~
Silver
"Us"…?

*Tsum peeking*

Sebek
Ah, Silver! The Tsum's moving around on your back!
Silver
I see, so that's why I felt a strange sensation on my back.
Lilia
Here now, Tsum. You've succeeded in giving Silver and Sebek a fright, now come back to my side.

*Tsum jump*

Silver
Regarding those frights actually…
Rumours about them have been spreading among our peers.
Wouldn't it be better to put a halt to the scaring at this stage?
Lilia
Hmm… Once the rumours circulate, the students are also likely to have their guards up in turn.
It's time we make our return to the dorm then, Tsum.

*Tsum reacting*

Lilia
…Hm? Why, shaking your head now, aren't you? You intend to continue still?

*Tsum zoom*

Sebek

AH! THE TSUM RAN AWAY! How blasphemous of you to defy Master Lilia's words!

Silver
It'd be terrible if it caused more trouble than it already has. We have to chase after it!
Lilia
For it to begin this time with a dramatic escape… The Tsum is certainly an intriguing fellow.

Courtyard

*Tsum moving*

Silver
Ah, the Tsum climbed up the apple tree!
Sebek
Alright, I'll go and catch it!
Lilia
Wait, you two! There's someone apart from the Tsum sitting on the bench next to the tree… Hm?
Sebek, Lilia & Silver

That's…!!

*Tsum lands*

Malleus
…Mm?
Did something fall on my head? Why do I have a feeling that something similar has happened to me before…

*Tsum gets down*

Malleus
Ahh, I knew it. So it was the same little creature from that time.
But this one resembles Lilia rather than Sebek.
Lilia
Nice catch, Malleus!
Malleus
Hm? Lilia. And Silver and Sebek as well.
Malleus
I see now, so you dropped by to look for me together with the three of them. Was that it?
Silver
Lord Malleus, apologies for interrupting you while you're reading your book. The Tsum seems to have struck your head, were you hurt by any chance?
Malleus
Nay. It's nothing major.
Lilia
How was it, Malleus? This Tsum's a splitting image of me and very adorable, don't you agree?
Malleus
It certainly does resemble you. It has your elusiveness, even.

*Happy Tsum*

Sebek
Young Master! Regarding this Tsum's insolent…

I MEAN, its unbridled conduct! Should it be…so honoured as to be punished for its misconduct?!

1

*Happy Tsum*

Malleus
There is no need. That degree of mischief is not something that could even begin to anger me, after all.
Lilia
As expected of Malleus. You're so open minded.
But still, for you to go so far as to attempt scaring even Malleus himself? I say, you have quite the daring personality.
Kehehe, I truly can't get bored while I'm with you.

*Happy Tsum*

Notes

This episode is a direct follow up to Episode 6 (Interactions with Lilia Tsum I), the first line here being the last line of that episode.

  1. Here Sebek’s exact line is いや、奔放な振る舞いに対し、罰を与え……させていただくべきでしょうか！？, rephrasing his question twice in because he still couldn’t make up his mind on whether to address the Tsum with honorifics or without lmao. His distraught expression when Malleus says there’s no need afterwards just seals it　(Return to text)
