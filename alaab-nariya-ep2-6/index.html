I’ll Open An Umbrella For You!

Malleus
It is much greener here. Compared to the earlier hustle and bustle, it’s so quiet now that I’d believe my ears are playing tricks on me.
Grim
Hey! I can see a really huge park there!
Kalim
Wanna go on a walk together, everyone? If you cut across here, you’ll reach my house in no time~
Cater
Ah, that’d be great. I can probably snap some good pics too since it’s such a beautiful park ♪
Jamil
Understood. I will get the car to stop and we’ll make our way on foot from here.

Scalding Sands - Asim Park

Malleus
It is certainly scorching outside.
Trey
Yeah. Thankfully, the walkway has a roof, so we can walk comfortably under the shade.
Kalim
If you walk straight along this path, you’ll reach my house!
Trey
Really? That’s pretty simple.
Cater
Ah, there’s a fountain. Looks perfect for cooling off~
Its peacock sculpture looks cool too, doesn’t it? Sure looks Magicammable ♪

*Snap!*

Malleus
Oh, this is quite the fine fountain sculpture! It may be no gargoyle, but it is of great intrigue.
I shall examine it at a closer distance.
Jamil
Ah! Malleus, you’ll get wet if you do!
(Having the actual prince of Briar Valley get sopping wet would be terrible!)
I’ll open an umbrella for you! Please wait a-
…Hm? The fountain’s spray is avoiding Malleus himself!?
Malleus
Using magic for such a purpose is no grand feat.
That aside, are you alright, Viper? You look like a soaked mouse. No matter how warm it may be, you’ll catch a cold if you stay as is.
Jamil
(Doesn’t this make it seem like I was the one frolicking about…!?)
Trey
Jamil’s carrying an umbrella around even on such a sunny day…?
Cater
As one might expect from the ever-ready~
Kalim
I get you, Malleus! You also feel excited from seeing fountains, right?
I used to play in the water here with Jamil when we were kids~
Jamil
Yeah. I’d prepared both a towel to dry you off and an extra change of clothes back then.
Trey
So that umbrella was probably meant for keeping Kalim dry too…
Malleus
So peacock’s tail feathers are represented using the fountain’s spray. It is well done.
Jamil
The fountain gushes water out every hour.
At night in particular when the illumination lights it up, the sculpture will resemble a colourful peacock.
Malleus
What an interesting design.
Grim
Fnagh… Ever since I fell into that canal, my body’s all sticky and it feels gross…
I can’t take it anymore!!

SPLASH!!

Cater
Ahh!! Gri-gri leapt into the fountain basin!
Grim
Wahahaha!! It’s so cold, it feels great!
Jamil
Goodness… And I’d dried you off earlier, too…
Kalim
That looks like fun! Alright, I’ll also-
Jamil
Stop! Don’t contribute anymore to the list of troubles!
