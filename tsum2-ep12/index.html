Interactions with Deuce Tsum II

Heartslabyul Dorm - Kitchen

Ace
Good work y'all~ We're back from our walk with the Tsum.
Trey
Ohh, welcome back. We've already started with the Unbirthday Party prep here.
Deuce
Sorry for being late. We were chasing the Tsum all over the place and before we knew it, it was already this…

*Angery Tsum*

Deuce
Isn't it true that it's youre fault that we're late? …Gotta say there was also someone egging it on, though.
Ace
Wowzers, wonder who that could be~? Ah, I'm gonna paint the roses red now, so go look after your Tsum yourself.

*Ace leaves*

Deuce
Seriously… Anyway, I will also start helping out with the prep work!
Trey
Oh, no actually. You don't have to, Deuce. The headmaster told you to take care of the Tsum, right?
I heard that the Tsums can be quite a handful, so just focus on looking after it for today.
Deuce
No, it's fine! It's still my duty, please allow me to carry it out.
Even if the Tsum is around, I can't just sit around and not lend a hand with the prep work!

*Clink, Clank…!*

Deuce
What's this sound… Tsum?
Trey
Uhh… Oh. It's stacking the plates on its back… It's pretty good at maintaining its balance, huh.
Deuce
Could it be…that you want to help too?

*Tsum reacting*

Deuce
I see, then let's work together. I'll grab the plates from the shelves, so please carry them to the party venue.
Trey
Ah… Just so you know, don't push yourself, alright? Both of you, Deuce and Tsum.
Deuce
Yes sir! Understood.
Trey
I've finished frosting the sponge cake with whipped cream since it has cooled down thoroughly. Can I count on you to decorate it?
Heartslabyul Student A
Got it! Strawberries and sugar decorations are good enough, right?
Trey
Yeah. I'll leave it to you then. I'll go and check if the pie is done baking for a bit.
Deuce
Alright, this is the last one. You're surprisingly good, aren't you, Tsum? You managed to get all the plates over without a single one breaking.
Heartslabyul Student B
Hey, Deuce. The tea canister is all out of tea leaves… Do you remember where they're kept?
Deuce
They should be stored in one of the upper shelves if I remember correctly. Wait a moment, I'll grab it now so…

*Clench, clench…*

Deuce
What is it, Tsum? You're moving as if you're bending and stretching…

*Tsum jump*

Deuce
Wha-?! It jumped?!

*Sw~ay~*

Deuce
It managed to jump all the way to the shelf's handle… But it's taking everything it's got just to hang on, so it doesn't seem like it can open it.
Tsum, come down. I'll get it, so…

*Ka-thunk, kathunk-thunk!*

Deuce
Oi, don't just use brute strength to shake it open! The cans and shelves will fall!

*Cre-! Cr-Creak…!*

Heartslabyul Student A
…Eh? Don't tell me…
Deuce
The storage shelf is falling over…?! WhoaAA?!?!

*Sliiiiiiiiiip!! Clank!*

Deuce
Th-The shelf and canister got positioned like a see-saw…

*BZOOOOOOOP!*

Deuce
The Tsum got catapulted from the end of the shelf it was on because the storage shelf fell onto the opposite end?!
How many lucky stars had to line up for THAT to happen?! Hold up, there's a ceiling lamp along its trajectory…

*Squish!*

Deuce
The Tsum's desperately clinging onto the lamp with its stubby little arms so things are alright for now…right?
Heartslabyul Student B
No wait, the lamp is swaying because of the impact from the Tsum knocking it.
Cater
Heard something real loud coming from in here, are y'all doing okay? …What the! Why has the shelf collapsed?!
Deuce
Sir Diamond, please stay away! The lamp is swinging because the Tsum hit it and…

*Swing, swing…*

*Clang! Kathunk! CRASH!*

Cater
Wha-! A pot landed right in front of me?!
Deuce
I'm sorry, Sir Diamond…! I'll get this sorted out somehow, so!

*Quiver quiver…*

Cater
Say, Tsummy's been clinging onto the lamp with all it's got for a while but like… Isn't it reaching its limit real soon?
Deuce
Tsum, get down! I'll catch you so please!

*Tsum zooms*

Cater & Deuce

It leaped away right before it fell?!

*Boing!*

Deuce
But the Tsum bounced off the wall after that leap!

*Woooooooosh!*

*…Splat!*

Deuce
Ah… The Tsum…landed on top of the cake Sir Clover made…!
Heartslabyul Student A
And it's already frosted with whipped cream too…
Cater
A-Ahhhh yikes~… Isn't this, like, super duper awful…

*Psshhh…*

Deuce
Oi, don't shrink yourself down to hide! Ahh, what do we do… It's also too late to start over…
Trey
Everyone, are you all…done…?
Deuce
…Ah.

*Distressed Tsum faces*

Trey
…So, what sort of ruckus do we have here? Setting aside the bad feeling I'm having and the fact that the pie has been baked…
Why is there a fallen shelf, all the pots scattered on the floor, and a Tsum on the cake instead of sugar decorations?
Deuce

WE… WE'RE TERRIBLY SORRYYYYYY!!
