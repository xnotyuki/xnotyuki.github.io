I’ll Make Sure You Have the Time of Your Life

Mirror Chamber

Jamil
Good morning, Headmaster.
Crowley
And you as well, good morning.
Jamil
Thank you very much for allowing us to use the Mirror Chamber today.
Kalim
For real! It’s a lifesaver!
Crowley
It’s only natural since this is Mr. Asim’s time to shine after all. I do provide such help for students in need, you see.
Crowley
For I am so very gracious.
Do send my sincerest regards to your father, alright? I also look forward to any souvenirs you bring back.
Trey
As expected of the headmaster. There’s no way he’d just do us a favour and not expect anything in return.
Crowley
Move along, everyone, and refrain from idly chatting for too long. You see, it’d be poor manners to leave “him” waiting.
Jamil
“Him”? Ahh, you must be referring to Lilia. He’d arrived before us, it seems.
Crowley
Oh? He’d indeed arrived much earlier and has been waiting for all of you ever since…
Crowley
But I was not referring to Mr. Vanrouge.
Kalim
Eh? It’s not Lilia? Means it’s Silver, I guess?

*Footsteps*

Malleus
Good Morning.
Jamil

MALLEUS!?

Malleus
You will be departing for the Scalding Sands soon, correct?
I’ll be joining you as well.
Cater, Jamil, Grim & Trey

EHH!?

Jamil
The person Lilia invited was Malleus and not Silver!?
Cater
Lilia really went that way, huh. Didn’t expect this at all.
Malleus
As the crown prince, I have next to no opportunities to leave Briar Valley.
I want to broaden my experiences. However…
If I were back home, the senate would fuss over all sorts of matters.
Thus, Lilia invited me on this journey.
“You can at least enjoy a leisurely trip while you’re still a student now, right”… Was what he said.
Cater
How kind of Lilsy ♪
Kalim
Huh? But I don’t see Lilia here, though?
Malleus
Correct. He fell ill with a sudden stomach ache this morning.
Therefore, I alone will be joining you
Cater, Jamil, Grim & Trey

WHAAAAAT!!!???

Malleus
According to Lilia, it seems that he was unlucky with his food yesterday.
Lilia said while rolled up in bed that it would be a good opportunity for me, and to have fun even if I’m on my own.
Although this is my first time having no escorts while travelling, I will make sure to thoroughly enjoy the Scalding Sands for Lilia’s sake as well.
Jamil
First time travelling without escorts…!?
Kalim
It’s really unfortunate that Lilia couldn’t join us, but…
I’ll make sure you have the time of your life, Malleus! Enjoy the Scalding Sands to your heart’s content and in Lilia’s stead too!
Jamil

Wait a minute!!!

Look, Kalim… Malleus is part of the royalty in Briar Valley.
Kalim
Mm? Yeah, and what of it?
Jamil
“What of it”…?
Without Lilia to watch over Malleus, what do you plan to do if something were to happen to him!?
Diplomatic issues might happen! It’s beyond what we can do!
Malleus
I’m not so fragile that you need to fret over me.
Jamil
Th-That might be the case, but…
Malleus
What’s the matter, Viper?
That expression of yours… Could it be that my presence here is a bother to you?
Jamil
No! N-no, no way, absolutely not! Why would I think that when you took such pains to join us…
Malleus
I thought I was being invited… Perhaps I was mistaken? Am I imposing myself on everyone here?
Jamil
Th-That’s not the case at all…!
Trey
So, uhh… Is it just me or do you guys also hear the thunder crackling in the distance?
Cater
I can hear it too~… And to think the weather was fine up till now.
Grim
Greenhorns’1 expression’s getting scarier and scarier…!
Jamil
(Oh man… Malleus’ mood is getting progressively worse…)
Crowley
It seems that Mr. Draconia had been standing here, waiting, for three hours. That was what he’d told me when I asked, at least.
Cater
Whoa! So Malleus must’ve been super duper excited for this!
Kalim
Hey, Jamil. Why are you overthinking this?
We’re just bringing our friends along to our birthplace, after all. Isn’t that normal?
Malleus
Exactly. Is there really a problem if I were to visit a dear schoolmate’s hometown?
Jamil
N-no, there’s no problem, but…
Trey
Outrageous logic is one thing, but Jamil is backed up in a corner slowly… What a rare sight to behold.
Cater
Malleus and Kalim are so carefree while being oblivious to the hard work of those around them, huh~
Jamil
…I-It can’t be helped, then. I suppose we have to let Malleus join us on his own.
Cater
This is quite fun… not. Things sure aren’t looking great ♪
Trey
You… You’re just thinking there’d be good photos to take or something, right? And here I am, having a bad feeling about this trip.
Crowley
Now, bon voyage, everyone!
Crowley
Oh right, if any trouble crops up during your trip, I will have no part in it at all, understood!?
Jamil
Seems like this year’s Yasmina River Fireworks Festival will be full of ups and downs…
Grim
Let’s go to the Scalding Sands, then!
