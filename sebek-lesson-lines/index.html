Sebek - Lesson Lines

Posted on 21st Mar 2023 (Tuesday) Edited on 12th Dec 2023 (Tuesday) In Twisted Wonderland , Lesson Lines , Sebek

Contains: History, Flight, Alchemy

I also did Silver’s Lesson lines here

History

Good

Morning

Sebek
I'm ready anytime.
I'm not…sleepy!
Humans are shallow.

Noon

Sebek
I have been enlightened.
The Young Master is even more splendid.
Feline! You're being noisy!

Afternoon

Sebek
Yield to the king.
The Young Master creates history.
Is Silver dozing off?

Great

Sebek
I want to be closer to the Young Master.
How intriguing.
This is a piece of cake!
Kuh! The pen nib got damaged!1

Perfect

Sebek
Easy.
I have no use for weaklings.
Don't look down on me, human.
I'll get everything right, I'll show you!1

Special Perfect

Sebek
This is my answer.
How old is the headmaster?
I'm not gonna just sit back and get thrown into disarray!

Flight

Good

Morning

Sebek
It's a good morning.
I won't lose to Silver.
I'm not afraid of getting injured.

Noon

Sebek
I can't turn smoothly.
Count on me for speed.
My stomach growled.

Afternoon

Sebek
I will recognise the coach's physical abilities.
The problem is my control.
Practise until you master it.

Great

Sebek
Ah! Young Master!
I feel like jumping for joy.
I've got no weak spots.

Perfect

Sebek
Nobody can keep up.
Great!
Are you racing me?

Special Perfect

Sebek
Headmaster, what are you here for?
Don't stand in my way!
You're all too slow!

Alchemy

Good

Morning

Sebek
Such a sullen look you've got this early in the morning.
Master Lilia is observing…
I am handy.
I'm gonna do it!
Eternal life and eternal youth2, huh…

Noon

Sebek
Have I surpassed Silver?
The colour looks unappetising…
I'm hungry…
I'm not a dog!
Have you read the annotations?

Afternoon

Sebek
I see.
Humans are greedy.
Gold can't even be used to fill stomachs.
This gem is befitting of the Young Master.
Keep quiet and listen in class!

Great

Sebek
There's nothing I can't handle.
Please praise me!
You think I'd fail at something of this calibre?
Hmph! Simple.
Have a look at the power of Briar Valley!

Perfect

Sebek
Young Master, please accept this.
This is nothing.
The Young Master would have done this.
How's that? Perfect, isn't it!
Not done yet?

Special Perfect

Sebek
Keep cool… Calm down.
No one can best the Young Master.
Hngh! I'm being watched.
I'll produce results that bring no shame to our dorm!
The compounding was flawless, wasn't it?

Notes

  1. These lines were added in the First Anniversary of the game.　(Return to text)

  1. A very minor nitpick on my end, 不老不死 is generally translated as immortality, but I don’t really like that because I find that immortality is not the same as unageing.　(Return to text)
