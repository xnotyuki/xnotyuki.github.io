Just How Big is It?!

Scalding Sands - Silk City

Kalim
Well then~ Before we get to the sightseeing, let’s head over to my house first!
Grim
Is your house far from here?
It’s so hot and I’m exhausted… I’d hate it if it takes hours or somethin’!
Kalim
It won’t take that much time. You can actually see my house from here.
Trey
That’s a lifesaver. How many minutes would it take roughly to walk over?
Kalim
Hm? You wanna walk? In that case…it’ll probably take three to four hours, I think?
Cater
Huh?! It still takes that much time to reach even though you said your house is visible from here?
Trey
Since it’s visible, then tell us. Which of the buildings is your house?
Kalim
See that one on the high ground? That’s my house.
Trey, Cater and Grim
THAT’S THE ONE!?
Cater
WHAT!? It’s so clearly visible even though it’s that far away! Just how big is it?!
Trey
It feels more like a theme park or a castle than a house…
Jamil
The Asim residence serves somewhat like a symbol for Silk City and it is widely known.
If you were to board a taxi and tell the driver to head to the Asim residence, they can and will bring you directly to it.
Kalim
There are tour buses stopping by too since visitors come on a daily basis for tours.
Trey
Truly a tourist attraction…
Malleus
But how will we be going there?
I don’t mind having to fly over, but…
Jamil
We’ve called for a car to pick us up, so please don’t worry.
Kalim
Oh, there it is!

*car pulls over*

Kalim
Okay, get in, guys.
Cater
…Wh-What’s with this huge car!?
Trey
I’ve never seen such a long car before. Just how long is it!?
Jamil
It spans 15 metres. It is custom-made, complete with its own refrigerator and massage chairs.
All that is because this is a limited-edition car that is used for picking up important guests.
Kalim
Ma and Pa prepared the car for us after I told them that I’d be bringing my schoolmates along.
Doing this much is normal, right? It’s for my dear friends to ride, after all.
Trey
It’s probably fitting for Malleus who’s a royal, but for the rest of us it’s really… Don’t you agree, Cater?
Cater
Ehhh~ What's wrong? Just get in, Trey.
You’d never get to step foot in a celeb’s car if such opportunities didn’t exist, right? Everything’s an experience, so don’t sweat it.

*Snap!*

Cater
Mm, good!
#beststart #celebjk #sparkly #scaldingsands sorta vibes!
I’ll post it later ♪
Trey
Later? Are you really okay with not posting now?
Cater
Well… It’s cuz we’re off school grounds now and travelling with Kalim and Malleus, y’know?
Don’t you think it’d be better to avoid letting others know our locations through live updates?
If Kalim got kidnapped or something because of the locations displayed on my posts, I’ll definitely end up being hated by Jamil.
Trey
I see… So it’s for safety purposes after all. I didn’t think of that myself, so I’ll also be careful from here on.
Cater
To make up for that, I’ll just spend all the extra time I have to edit loads of pics of Trey-Trey and then post them all later~
Trey
Wait, enough with mine… Our dorm mates are gonna make fun of me again.
Kalim
Let’s go now! We’re gonna reach my house in the blink of an eye.
